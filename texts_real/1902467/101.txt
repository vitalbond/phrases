<div><p>W3C released today a Positive Work Environment Framework that includes a <a href="http://www.w3.org/Consortium/cepc/">Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct</a> and accompanying <a href="http://www.w3.org/Consortium/pwe/#Procedures">procedures</a>. Both guidelines are now in effect and govern our work environment.</p>
<p>W3C is a growing and global community where participants from all horizons choose to work together. Our Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct defines a set of community principles and promotes high standards of professional practice. It also acts as a vehicle for better identity of the organization which celebrates its <a>20th anniversary</a> this month.</p></div>
