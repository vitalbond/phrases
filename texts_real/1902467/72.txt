<div><p>The <a href="http://www.w3.org/WAI/RD/">Research and Development Working Group (RDWG)</a> will hold an online symposium on 3 December 2014 to explore technologies, applications, concepts, and solutions that help people with disabilities orient themselves, explore, and navigate through the physical world. The <a href="http://www.w3.org/WAI/RD/2014/way-finding/cfp.html">Call for Papers</a> is open until 20 October 2014. Learn more about the <a href="http://www.w3.org/WAI/RD/2014/way-finding/Overview.html">Accessible Way-Finding Using Web Technologies Symposium</a> and the <a href="http://www.w3.org/WAI/">Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI)</a>.</p></div>
