<div><p>The W3C Advisory Committee has elected Mark Nottingham (Akamai Technologies) to the <a href="http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/">W3C Technical Architecture Group (TAG)</a>. The mission of the TAG is to build consensus around principles of Web architecture and to interpret and clarify these principles when necessary, to resolve issues involving general Web architecture brought to the TAG, and to help coordinate cross-technology architecture developments inside and outside W3C.</p>
