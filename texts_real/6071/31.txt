<h3>Weird Al Debuts “Happy” Parody Called “Tacky”</h3>
<p>Weird Al Yankovic released the first music video for his <a title="#8videos8days" href="https://twitter.com/alyankovic/status/483281985024962560" target="_blank">#8videos8days</a> project earlier this week.  <a title="Al's video 'Tacky'" href="http://news.iheart.com/articles/trending-475573/watch-weird-al-debuts-happy-paraody-12563056" target="_blank">Al’s video <em>Tacky</em></a> is a parody of Pharrell’s popular <em>Happy</em>. The video features guest appearances from Aisha Tyler and Jack Black, and displays lots of clashing colors, sequined fanny packs, and tasteless dance moves. Over 50% of content about Weird Al’s new videos was shared to Facebook.</p>
<h3>World Cup Final Was Most Talked-About Sports Event Ever on Facebook</h3>
<p>Sunday’s FIFA World Cup Final resulted in a win for Germany and <a title="record-breaking social media interactions" href="http://www.adweek.com/news/technology/yesterdays-world-cup-final-was-most-talked-about-sports-event-ever-facebook-158899" target="_blank">record-breaking social media interactions</a>. Over 280 million game-related interactions on Facebook were generated by 88 million people. The previous record was set by the 2013 Super Bowl with 245 interactions.</p>
<h3>LeBron James Re-Joins the Cleveland Cavaliers</h3>
<p>LeBron James started his professional career in 2003 with the Cleveland Cavaliers, and in 2010, became a member of the Miami Heat. Lebron went on to become the youngest player in NBA history to score 20,000 career points in January 2013. James opted out of his contract with the Heat on June 25 and became a free agent on July 1. On July 11, he revealed that he intended to <a title="re-join the cavaliers" href="http://abcnews.go.com/Sports/lebron-james-back-cleveland-joins-cavaliers/story?id=24493464" target="_blank">re-join the Cavaliers</a>. Facebook was the most-used social service for sharing the LeBron news, making up 43% of shared content.</p>
<h3>Man Endures Sweltering Heat in Viral “Hot Car Challenge” Video</h3>
<p>Terry Bartley was motivated to mimic what it feels like for a child to be left in a hot car after the death of 22-month-old Cooper Harris in Atlanta. Bartley <a title="sat in his car with the windows rolled up" href="http://abcnews.go.com/US/north-carolina-man-endures-sweltering-heat-viral-hot/story?id=24548746" target="_blank">sat in his car with the windows rolled up</a> as the outside temperature reached the upper 80′s. He’s hoping to raise awareness for parents to stop leaving their children in the car unattended.</p>
<h3>3 Year Old and WWII Veteran Form Special Friendship</h3>
<p>The Rychner’s lived next to Erling Kindem for nearly a decade without knowing each other well. That all changed when their son Emmett, who was 2 at the time, <a title="visited Erling and forged a bond over the tomatoes in his garden" href="http://cnsnews.com/mrctv-blog/eric-scheiner/3-year-old-and-wwii-veteran-form-special-friendship" target="_blank">visited Erling and forged a bond over the tomatoes in his garden</a>. The two now race lawnmowers, play croquet, and fix bikes. Both the Rychner’s and Erling are moving from the neighborhood, and the pair’s heartwarming bond will be tested.</p>
