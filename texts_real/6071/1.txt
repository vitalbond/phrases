<h3>Mother&rsquo;s Day Turns 100: Its Surprisingly Dark History</h3>
<p>Speaking of Mother&rsquo;s Day, National Geographic taught us the <a title="holiday's dark history" href="http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2014/05/140508-mothers-day-nation-gifts-facts-culture-moms/" target="_blank">holiday&rsquo;s dark history</a>. A holiday now stereotyped with flowers and brunches was founded for women to remember fallen soldiers and promote peace. The first Mother&rsquo;s Day celebrated as we know it was in 1908.
