<p>We rolled out a list of new widgets we want to hear your opinion on. <h3>Follow Badge</h3>
<p>This badge would pull in your Twitter profile and enable viewers to follow your handle, helping you to grow your Twitter audience. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/follow-badge" target="_blank">Vote for the Follow Badge</a></p>
<h3>Spark Line</h3>
<p>This widget has the same colorful spark line that Mashable offers, showing shares and highlighting what’s popular and trending for each post. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/spark-line" target="_blank">Vote for the Spark Line</a></p>
<h3>Hero Recommended Widget</h3>
<p>This recommendation widget appears front and center of a page so that none of your visitors run the risk of missing content based on what’s trending and relevant. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/hero-recommended-widget" target="_blank">Vote for the Hero Recommended Widget</a></p>
<h3>Pinterest or Instagram Feed</h3>
<p>Showcase your style with a feed of your Pinterest pins or your Instagram photos that could be placed on select pages of your site. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/pinterest-or-instagram-feed" target="_blank">Vote for the Pinterest or Instagram Feed</a></p>
<h3>Product Price Recommendation</h3>
<p>This e-commerce focused tool would show related products along with a price to help you increase conversions. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/product-recommendations" target="_blank">Vote for the Product Price Recommendation</a></p>
<h3>Ad Support Recommended Widgets</h3>
<p>Access Pro for free. In exchange, adding this widget would show advertisements along with your recommended content. <a href="https://www.addthis.com/dashboard#gallery/vote/ad-supported-pro" target="_blank">Vote for this tool</a></p>
