<h3>91-Year-Old to Run Sunday&rsquo;s San Diego Marathon</h3>
<p>Harriette Thompson is not your typical 91-year-old. &nbsp;The cancer survivor and Charlotte, NC resident <a title="raced in her 15th marathon" href="http://www.runnersworld.com/runners-stories/91-year-old-to-run-sundays-san-diego-marathon" target="_blank">raced in her 15th marathon</a> on Sunday at the San Diego Rock n Roll Marathon. &nbsp;She completed the race in 7 hours and 7 minutes, which was a record time for a woman 90 years and older. &nbsp;A whopping 87% of content about the marathoner was shared to Facebook.</p>
<h3>Gerbils and Fanny Packs &ndash; Ricky Gervais Plays Word Games with Jimmy Fallon</h3>
<p>Ricky Gervais, the English comedian and actor, was a guest on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon this week. &nbsp;<a title="Fallon challenged Gervais to a game of Word Sneak" href="http://www.radiotimes.com/news/2014-06-03/gerbils-nip-slips-and-fanny-packs---ricky-gervais-plays-word-games-with-jimmy-fallon" target="_blank">Fallon challenged Gervais to a game of Word Sneak</a>, where they had to secretly slip words into a conversation. &nbsp;Words included gerbils, fanny pack, sloppy joe, and squeegee.</p>
<h3>Dolphins Guide Scientists to Rescue Suicidal Girl</h3>
<p>A group of researchers were following a school of dolphins near Los Angeles, CA. &nbsp;One dolphin suddenly changed direction and the rest of the dolphins followed. &nbsp;The <a title="dolphins were headed toward an 18-year-old girl" href="http://newswatch.nationalgeographic.com/2014/05/29/dolphins-guide-scientists-to-rescue-suicidal-girl/" target="_blank">dolphins were headed toward an 18-year-old girl </a>who, the team later found out, was attempting to commit suicide. &nbsp;While there isn&rsquo;t sufficient evidence to conclude that dolphins save humans, their actions did help bring medical care to this girl.</p>

<p>Former Microsoft chief executive <a title="Steve Ballmer purchased the LA Clippers" href="http://abcnews.go.com/Sports/steve-ballmer-bids-2b-clippers/story?id=23921625" target="_blank">Steve Ballmer purchased the LA Clippers</a> for $2B from the Sterling family. &nbsp;Sterling&rsquo;s wife Shelley Sterling was the sole trustee and had the power to deal directly with interested parties. &nbsp;An agreement was signed late last week and sent directly to the NBA for approval. &nbsp;Twitter sharing made up 32% of the content shared about the transaction.</p>
