<div><p>Tomorrow, the second part of the episodic DLC single player expansion  for <em>Bioshock: Infinite,</em> <em>Burial at Sea Episode 2</em>, will be released.  Here are the full details on the final DLC episode for <em>Bioshock: Infinite</em>, courtesy of the <a href="http://twitter.com/irrationalgames">Irrational Games Twitter Feed</a>.</p>
<p>Xbox 360: 5AM EDT<br>
PC: 1-3PM EDT<br>
PSN NA: 3-7 PM EDT<br>
PSN Europe/Asia: Wednesday</p>
<p>The DLC is expected to weigh in between 6 and 10GB, depending on your platform.  PC users will have to download a small 12MB patch through Steam in order to play the DLC.</p>
