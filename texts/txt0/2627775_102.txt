<div><p>First announced at E3 2014, inFAMOUS: First Light is a standalone downloadable game where you take control of Abagail “Fetch” Walker in a prequel story to <em>inFAMOUS: Second Son</em>.</p>
<p>Those who fondly remember using Fetch’s neon powers will get the chance to use them once again in this game.  Developer Sucker Punch Studios has promised that Fetch had a few neon powers she did not give Delsin in the original game, and players will get the chance to use then in <em>First Light</em>.</p>
<p>Sony currently has the game available for preorder on the Playstation Store. Anyone with a PS4 can purchase it right now for a price of $14.99 US.  They are offering free exclusive DLC to anyone who preorders the game.</p>
<p><em>inFAMOUS: First Light</em> is coming August 26th, 2014 exclusively to the Playstation 4.</p><br><a rel="nofollow" href="http://feeds.wordpress.com/1.0/gocomments/gamexcess.wordpress.com/7286/"><img alt="" border="0" src="http://feeds.wordpress.com/1.0/comments/gamexcess.wordpress.com/7286/"></a> <img alt="" border="0" src="http://stats.wordpress.com/b.gif?host=gamexcess.net&#38;blog=12380894&#38;post=7286&#38;subd=gamexcess&#38;ref=&#38;feed=1" width="1" height="1"></div>
