<div><p>If you’re like me and were blown away by Konami’s E3 2014 trailer for <em>Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain</em> and <em>had</em> to see more, you are in luck.  The guys over at Konami have posted up the gameplay footage from their E3 2014 demonstration.</p>
<p>Make no mistake this is over thirty minutes of <em>Metal Gear Solid V</em> gameplay.  The game takes place in an open world environment over two hundred times bigger than the environment in <em>Metal Gear Solid: Ground Zeroes</em>.  The developers have also mentioned the game is currently running in 1080p at 60hz on a Playstation 4.</p>
