<p><a href="http://moviepoke.net/">Moviepoke</a></p>
<p>Moviepoke is a new beta startup that aims to provide you upcoming movies trailer at any place which you can bookmark.</p>
<p>When your bookmark movies get released in cinema halls or on dvds then you will get alerts about it as well.</p>

<p>2.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.vidspoke.com/">Vidspoke</a></p>
<p>Vidspoke is a new beta startup that aims to help you make video commercials ads on youtube in just $20.</p>
<p>All you need to do is just choose a personality you wish to produce your promotional video, skit, testimonial, or whatever you are looking to produce. Then choose the options you want for your video, and shoot.</p>
