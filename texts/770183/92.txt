<div><p><strong>Zula:</strong></p>
<p><strong></strong><strong style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;">Zula </strong><span style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;">is a mobile solution for team communication that aims to offer all the tools a team needs for ongoing interaction: instantly joining a conversation,</span><strong style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;"> </strong><span style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;">discussing</span><strong style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;"> </strong><span style="text-align: right; font-size: 13px;">ideas, sharing content, taking a poll, and connectin over a conference call.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: right;">
</p><p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/team-communication.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35640" title="team communication" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/team-communication.png" alt="" width="173" height="239"></a></p>
<p><strong>How It Works:</strong></p>
<p>All you need to do is just download Zula from the App Store or Google Play, or if you have already been invited to a Zula, simply follow the link in the invitation to install Zula.</p>
<p>Once installed then you can either use your existing Facebook or Google accounts to quickly log in to Zula, or create a brand new Zula account if you want to keep it separate.</p>
<p><strong>Best Part Is:</strong></p>
<p>Zula provides all the tools needed for focused business conversations, while <a href="http://visionwiz.net/2010/02/whatsapp-is-a-smartphone-2-smartphone-instant-messenger/">Whatsapp</a> focuses on social interaction.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Zula provides teams with an ongoing central stream of conversation, easy access to shared files, shared events, polling and one touch group calling.</span></p>
