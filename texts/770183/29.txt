<div><p><strong>Must Use Apps For Journalists Or Bloggers&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://itunes.apple.com/app/zinio/id364297166?ign-mpt=uo%3D6&amp;mt=8"><br>
Zinio</a></strong></p>
<p>Zinio is great app for people who love magazines. By using this apps you can access thousands of titles, tens of thousands of issues, and millions of pages of the world&rsquo;s best digital content.</p>
<p>You can download and read your digital issues for online and offline access, archive back issues, and synchronize your library to multiple iPhones, iPads, and other devices.</p>
<p><a href="http://itunes.apple.com/app/flipboard/id358801284?ign-mpt=uo%3D6&amp;mt=8"><br>
Flipboard</a></p>
<p>Flipboard is a beautiful way to&nbsp;<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/08/four-quick-way-to-read-and-listen-news-every-morning-while-on-go/">flip through the news</a>, photos, videos, and updates your friends are sharing on Facebook, Twitter, Google Reader, LinkedIn, Flickr, and Instagram.</p>
<p><a href="http://itunes.apple.com/app/feedhopper-rss-reader/id361881998?ign-mpt=uo%3D6&amp;mt=8"><br>
FeedHopper<br></a></p>
<p>FeedHopper RSS Reader is built from the ground up to be an<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/10/no-time-for-reading-your-rss-feedsthen-listen-your-rss-feeds-via-webtalks/">&nbsp;iPad-centric RSS,</a>&nbsp;Twitter, podcast, and Web viewer.</p>
<p>With the press of a button, all of your blog and news sites are checked for updates, which are then downloaded into feedHop for searching and reading.</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/news-apps.jpg"><img title="news apps" alt="" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/news-apps.jpg" width="183" height="275"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://itunes.apple.com/app/pulse-news-for-ipad/id371088673?ign-mpt=uo%3D6&amp;mt=8"><br></a><a href="https://itunes.apple.com/in/app/linkedin-pulse-news-insights/id377594176?mt=8">Pulse news apps</a></p>
<p>Pulse&nbsp;news apps&nbsp;would help you to take your favorite websites and transforms them into a colorful and interactive mosaic. Tap on an article to see a clean and elegant view of the news story.</p>
<p>By using this apps you can save stories for reading later across all platforms or sync them&nbsp;<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2009/11/with-instapaper-read-later-all-useful-information/">with Instapaper, Read it Later&nbsp;</a>and Evernote.</p>
<p><a href="http://itunes.apple.com/app/fluent-news-reader-free-online/id312886230?ign-mpt=uo%3D6&amp;mt=8"><br>
Fluent news</a></p>
<p>Fluent<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/03/convert-your-rss-feeds-into-videos-get-personalized-television-style-news/">&nbsp;news displays</a>&nbsp;complete articles without load-time delays, text pinching or leaving the application.</p>
<p>Fluent News aggregates made-for-mobile versions of top news sources into a single, intelligently organized, fully customizable mobile newspaper.</p>
<p>It allows you to create up to 3 of your own custom sections based on keyword searches.</p></div>
