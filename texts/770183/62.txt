<div><p><strong>4 Startups From Chicago</strong></p>
<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.getchitter.com/">Chitter</a></p>
<p>Chitter is a new startup that rewards you with free offers and special discounts for doing something you already do: posting photos to your social media!</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 2.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://www.foxtrotchicago.com/">Foxtrot</a></span></p>
<p>Foxtrot is a new startup that aims to handpicked &amp; curate best food menus and deliver it within one hour at your location.</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/startup.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35505" title="startup" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/startup.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="99"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 3.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://www.hoodle.net/">Hoddle</a></span></p>
<p>Hoddle is a new startup that aims to provide home buyers across the U.S. with the very best tools to easily find and compare information on building a new home, while at the same time showcasing builders, subdivisions, product/ service suppliers, and Realtors all across the country.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 4.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://standardhealth.org/">StandardHealth</a></span></p>
<p>StandardHealth is a new startup that aims to provide free health info &amp; electronic health profile(ehp)via sms.</p>
<p>Each user on Standard health gets a EHP created for them. This shortens doctor visit times as all of the basic info is already available. All health visits are also logged to ensure treatment plans are working.</p>
<p>Users can store their food and exercise data by texting Standard Health with the type of food or exercise. This allows them, doctors, and researchers to identify problems and mitigate them quickly.</p>
