<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.rciprocity.com/?test=true">Rciprocity</a></p>
<p>Rciprocity is a social luxury brand and platform that raises money to support women in business through selling unique handbags.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Every week they feature a woman, aka, passion story, to raise funding and awareness for women in need around the world. They partner with designers for handbags. They also design and manufacture Rciprocity originals.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 2.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://www.unityreports.com/">UnityReports</a></span></p>
<p>UnityReports is a SaaS analytics solution that merges data from your e-commerce and Google Analytics in order to create dashboards that help you grow your business.</p>
<p>3.</p>
<p><a href="https://dayboard.co/">Dayboard</a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 1.17em;">Dayboard lets you find out what your team is working on and helps everyone complete their most important tasks each day.</span></p>
