<div><p><strong>Chui:</strong></p>
<p>Chui &nbsp;is&nbsp; a new smart device that aims&nbsp; to help you &nbsp;<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/10/now-lock-open-your-door-from-smartphone/">monitor your d</a><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/10/now-lock-open-your-door-from-smartphone/">oor</a> while you are away from </span><a style="font-size: 13px;" href="http://visionwiz.net/2013/07/new-virtual-keyaugust-smart-lock-to-lock-your-home/">home</a><span style="font-size: 13px;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Through its face recognition technologies you can identify person who is knocking on your door and communicate with him via your mobile apps.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">In addition to reading faces Chui can also read QR codes. You can enable QR Code recognition as a second layer of security.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Which would allow you to hold up a code to the device (visible on your phone or on a piece of paper), and Chui will read the code along with your face.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Chui will only return a positive match if both your face and the code match the information on record. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Meaning that even if someone obtains your QR code it is useless without your face. QR codes can be set, requested, and generated from their Web and Mobile applications</span></p>
