<div><p><strong>3 Internet of Things Based Startups</strong></p>
<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.iotera.com/">Iotera</a></p>
<p>Iotera is  a new startup that based on Internet of Things (IOT), which refers to a time when location data on every man-made object will be available.</p>
<p>In an IoT world, companies will never have to run inventory on products, parents will always know if their kids were in danger, and wallets will never be lost.</p>
<p>2.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.smartthings.com/">SmartThings</a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">SmartThings is a new startup that aims to help you control all things(devices at home) via your smartphone.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/internet-of-things1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35628" title="internet of things" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/internet-of-things1.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="99"></a><br></span></p>
<p>The SmartThings Hub and family of devices offer everything you need to start experiencing a safer, smarter home.</p>
<p>All you need to do is just  open the box, follow the on-screen instructions in its easy-to-use app, and you can begin using your smartphone like a remote to control your connected things in as little as 15 minutes.</p>
<p>3.</p>
<p><a href="http://hitekelec.com/myknut/">Knut</a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Knut is a new startup based on Wi-Fi enabled sensor hub that enables you to monitor your environment in real time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> </span><span style="font-size: 13px;">You can use Knut to monitor the temperature of your home or aquarium, tell if someone has opened your liquor cabinet or refrigerator door, and thousands home or aquarium, tell if someone has opened your liquor cabinet or refrigerator door, and thousands </span><span style="font-size: 13px;">more uses.</span></p>
