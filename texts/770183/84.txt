<div><p><strong>Piggyback:</strong></p>
<p>Piggyback is a new startup that aims to help you provide &nbsp;<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2014/03/washio-on-demand-laundry-service-at-your-door/">same day deli</a><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2014/03/washio-on-demand-laundry-service-at-your-door/">very </a>of items&nbsp; through door to drop and door to pick up service.</span></p>
<p>Piggyback charge&rsquo;s a fee of $1.00 per mile for same day local door to door delivery within a 50 mile radius within San Diego County.</p>
<p>If you would like to ship something out of their service area using its discount rates with FEDEX or UPS, they pick up, pack and drop off your packages at the closest shipping outlet for free. All you have to pay is for their shipping fee&rsquo;s.</p>

<p><strong>How It Works:</strong></p>
<p>All you need to do is just download the android smartphone app by requesting a link here<a href="http://www.piggyback.co/download"> download app request</a>.The link will be sent directly to your email.</p>
<p>Now open the app on your smartphone and take a pictureof the item you wish to ship, include your name, phone number, item description&nbsp;and select Same Day Local or Standard Shipping.&nbsp;And click on the request a delivery buddy.</p>
