<div><p><strong>Machinio:</strong></p>
<p>Machinio is a new startup that aims to connect buyers and sellers from around the world for machinery equipment.</p>
<p>Machinio is the only search engine for buying and selling used industrial equipment and machinery.</p>
<p>By using their platform you can easily locate second-hand equipment.</p>
<p>Using Machinio, you can search over 1.25 million listings from thousands of websites in one location.</p>

<p><strong>Easy To Use:</strong></p>
<p>For using it, you must contact the seller directly either by filling out the contact form or by visiting the seller&rsquo;s website.</p>
<p>Payment must be handled directly with the seller. Machinio is the platform for connecting buyers and sellers, but is not the seller.</p>
