<div><p><strong>3 Beta Startups For SignUp</strong></p>
<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.roburir.com/">Robur</a></p>
<p>Robur&rsquo;s unique tool Pecuniosus allows you to analyse and compare company financial trends with the click of a button.</p>
<p>They source all their data, by hand, from company reports so that you always get accurate and up-to-date figures, as well as saving you countless hours of due diligence, leaving you with more time to make better informed and more effective investment decisions.</p>

<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 2.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://datmachine.co/">DatMachine</a></span></p>
<p>DatMachine is a new startup that aims to identify which types of visitors are more likely to convert in paying customers, discover which parts of your website influence customer decision making process the most( both positively and negatively) and find unexpected patterns in visitors&rsquo; behavior.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 3.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://referralrock.com/">ReferralRock</a></span></p>
<p>Referral Rock is a new startup that aims to help you&nbsp;build, launch and track your customer referral program.</p>
