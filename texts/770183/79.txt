<div><p><strong>Six Smart Device To &nbsp;Make Your Daily Life Simple</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/02/now-you-can-communicate-with-your-device-via-twingz/">1.<br>
Twingz</a></p>
<p>Twingz wants to create a new world where any device(things) can communicate with us.</p>
<p>Things &ndash; for example electric devices &ndash; are enriched with communication features.</p>
<p>Users on the go can check back state and healthiness of devices that sit at home.</p>
<p>It means that now on wards you need not to worry about your devices as they would let you inform about their conditions itself .</p>
<p>2<a style="font-size: 13px;" href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/10/new-magic-finger-will-find-anything-that-your-eyes-cant-see-or-locate/">.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/10/new-magic-finger-will-find-anything-that-your-eyes-cant-see-or-locate/">Magic Finger</a></p>
<p>Magic Finger is a thimble-like device worn on the user&rsquo;s finger. It combines two optical sensors: a lower resolution, high speed sensor for tracking movement, and a higher-resolution camera for capturing detail.</p>
<p>Magic Finger can identify the object that users touch, tapping different surfaces can trigger different command executions.</p>
<p>3<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/03/create-a-link-between-online-offline-content-via-touchcode/">.<br>
TouchCode</a></p>
<p>Touchcode is an invisible electronic date tag that can be printed on nearly any surface &ndash; including paper, carton or foil &ndash; to establish a link between online and offline communications.</p>
<p>Consumers simply place a printed product with the embedded data tag on the screen of their device.</p>
<p>Then an app or website can instantly open up content, such as videos, websites, photos and more.</p>
<p>Touchcode tags can even be used to validate the authenticity of a ticket or a packaged product.</p>

<p>4<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/10/new-devicecrash-sensor-will-alert-you-when-your-friends-had-an-accident/">.<br>
ICEdot</a></p>
<p>ICEdot is an emergency identification and notification service that provides critical emergency contact and health information at the scene of an accident.</p>
<p>The service utilizes the internet, mobile applications and sms technology to quickly identify a patient, relay important medical information, provide and notify emergency contacts &ndash; in case of emergency.</p>
<p>Its Crash Sensor is a slim device when paired with the ICEdot app on a smart phone, the system is able to detect motion, changes in forces and impacts.</p>
<p>The device sends critical data to the app which sounds an alarm and initiates an emergency countdown.</p>
<p>5<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/09/now-communicate-with-your-home-appliance-or-device-through-ubi/">.<br>
Ubi</a></p>
<p>Ubi is a new voice control device that plugs into a wall and able to access almost anything you can access on the Internet through a wifi connection.</p>
<p>And it can control and monitor any appliance or device connected to it.</p>
<p>6<a href="http://visionwiz.net/2012/08/new-devicelapka-can-monitor-invisible-things-around-you/">.<br>
Lapka</a></p>
<p>Lapka is a new device that connects with your phone to measure, collect and analyze the hidden qualities of your surroundings.</p>
<p>This device detects electromagnetic fields (EMF), which can be caused by electronic hardware, telecommunication transmitters, or nearby power lines.</p>
<p>It could, for instance, suggest a spot at home with the least electromagnetic pollution to help you place your bed, desk or yoga mat.</p>
<p>It is highly sensitive and responds to the invisible world of particles, ions, molecules and waves, so it can analyze your surroundings and smartly combine the results into guideline values for your comfort</p>
