<p><a href="http://www.clearformat.com/">ClearFormat</a></p>
<p>ClearFormat is a very innovative tool that allows you to unify and attach your corporate identity to all outgoing emails.</p>
<p>In other words,ClearFormat presents your organization with a unique opportunity to use the numerous standard outgoing business emails that are delivered daily to your valued customers and business partners, by providing a compelling new dimension to corporate branding and marketing.</p>
<p>Email branding offered by ClearFormat gives companies the tool set to effectively manage and control a legally secure marketing and communications platform.<br><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/email-marketing2.jpg"><img title="email marketing" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/email-marketing2.jpg" alt="" width="228" height="221"></a><br>
All your outgoing emails can be monitored and tracked to give you a statistical breakdown on how successful your email marketing campaigns are doing.</p>

Contactology is an email marketing and online survey tool designed for experts and beginners.</p>
<p>Contactology offers an array of tools for beginners and experts.You can manage your contacts as a list, group or a dynamic list segment. They give you all the tools to manage contacts and start sending attractive email marketing and surveys.</p>

<p><a href="http://sendloop.com/">Sendloop</a></p>
<p>Sendloop is a professional yet easy-to-use email marketing and mail list management service for any kind of online/offline businesses.</p>
<p>Sendloop includes all professional features that you will need for your email marketing campaign combined with a very easy to use interface. In just a few minutes, you will get your account up and ready to send your first email campaign.</p>

<p>4.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.streamsend.com/">StreamSend</a></p>
<p>StreamSend is the most comprehensive and cost-effective permission-based e-mail marketing suite available.It&rsquo;s only email marketing applications that offers every customer a private IP address .</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s a web-based application that allows you to simply log-in to you account and create and send email campaigns.<br>
StreamSend does not require any software to be downloaded or installed on your computer.</p>
<p>They do not require that you sign a one-year contract, as most other robust applications do, and they offer a 30 day free trial so that you can see for yourself just how powerful a tool StreamSend can be.</p>

<p>5.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.ennect.com/">Ennect</a></p>
<p>Ennect Mail is web-based email marketing software that empowers you to send visually rich emails and analyze results.<br>
Ennect Mail can store up to 100,000 email addresses and up to 100,000 responses. You can add additional storage as needed. Ennect Mail comes standard with 2 Mb of image file storage for storing images.</p>

<p>6.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.cakemail.com/">CakeMail</a></p>
<p>CakeMail is white label email marketing platform. It&rsquo;s a true private label tool that uses a reseller&rsquo;s own domain name, even for the user interface. And it can be offered in any language.</p>
<p>For end-users, it&rsquo;s a great online tool for easily creating and managing newsletter mailings.<br>
For agencies, it&rsquo;s a true white label solution that lets you adopt CakeMail and market it as your own with total control over the design, branding and domain name.</p>
<p>For third-party developers, designers and translators, it&rsquo;s a platform that you can use to extend CakeMail&rsquo;s features with plugins, skins, and new interface languages.</p>
