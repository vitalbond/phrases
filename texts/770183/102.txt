<div><p><strong>4 Startups From <a href="http://bluestartups.com/">Blue Startups </a>Demo Days</strong></p>
<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.ingollow.com/">Ingollow</a></p>
<p>Ingollow is a new startup that aims to create authentic social relationships between the users (locals &#38; travelers) and the Ingollw businesses in a specific geographic area through social networking and a social commerce enterprise platform.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">2.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://www.oroeco.com/">Oroeco</a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">Oroeco, the world’s first service that rewards you and all your friends for taking action to fight climate change.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;">This startup will track your climate impacts  by automatically putting a carbon value on everything you buy, what you eat, how you get around, and the energy you use at home.</span><span style="font-size: 13px;">Then they add everything up, so you can see which decisions are bigger than others.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/bluestartupslogo2.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35689" title="bluestartupslogo2" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/bluestartupslogo2.png" alt="" width="267" height="103"></a><br></span></p>
<p>Oroeco  also gives you personalized tips for how you can save money along with the climate and they’ve partnered with <a href="http://impactcarbon.org/" target="_blank">Impact Carbon</a>, to eliminate your remaining climate footprint by helping provide clean cookstoves that save forests and lives in Africa.</p>
<p>Best of all, they make everything fun and social, connecting with Facebook to compare, collaborate, and compete with your friends and family.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 3.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="https://auth.cacvantage.com/">Vantage</a></span></p>
<p>Vantage is a new startup that aims to help our media and broadcast partners to tell better stories about the games, teams, and players they cover.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"> 4.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px;"><a href="http://www.peoplepowerco.com/">PeoplePower</a></span></p>
<p>PeoplePower is a new startup that aims to help you monitor your home while you are away from home via your smartphone.</p>
