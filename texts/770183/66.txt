<div><p><strong>5 Digital Ways To Monitor Your Health Online</strong></p>
<p>1.</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/09/now-monitor-your-blood-pressure-weight-via-iphone-appsihealth/">iHealth</a></p>
<p>iHealth &nbsp;aims to empower people to manage their health by providing easy to use devices and tools to test, track and share personal health data.</p>
<p>The iHealth Blood Pressure Monitoring System lets users measure and track their blood pressure and heart rate from the convenience of their iPhone, iPod touch and iPad.<br><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/health-apps.jpg"><img title="health apps" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/health-apps.jpg" alt="" width="275" height="183"></a><br>
The system comes with a blood pressure arm cuff and a portable, battery-powered dock allowing for quick and easy set up.The dock also doubles as a charging station for all three iOS devices.</p>
<p>2</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/07/know-which-thing-leads-you-to-maximum-stress-via-stress-tracking-tool/">Stress Pile</a></p>
<p>Stress Pile is a very useful tool to monitor your stress level.Stress Pile can benefit anyone who is looking to pin point and visually see whats stressing them the most at that moment or over time.</p>
<p>By using this Stress pile, you have the chance to easily save your stress, track your Stress and find the ways to treat your stress in the right order of your choice.</p>
<p>StressPile will play a personal audio recording or image when your stress is highest and guide you back to the present moment.<br>
You can use StressPile for everything from school, work to your personal life and everything else in between.</p>
<p>3</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/09/qa-based-health-apps-for-iphoneipad-android-devices/">HealthTap<br></a></p>
<p>HealthTap&rsquo;s free web and mobile applications enable 24/7 access to personalized, relevant, and trusted health information from thousands of leading doctors, helping you find the best care and make better decisions about your health and well-being.</p>
<p>How?</p>
<p>HealthTap would connect you with thousands of best physicians (around you) providing quick answers to health questions &ndash; anytime, anywhere.</p>
<p>HealthTap provides you with answers to questions from U.S.-licensed physicians, including the best doctors in your area.</p>
<p>4</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2011/06/jet-lag-job-stress-mood-swings-then-use-valkee-light-emitting-earplugs/">Valkee</a></p>
<p>Valkee headset might help you to prevent your mood swings &amp; stress problems. The Valkee bright light headset activates the human brain and prevent your mood swings.</p>
<p>Valkee&rsquo;s brain stimulation headset is based on new findings in brain science and bright light therapy.</p>
<p>The Valkee light emitting earplugs prevent and treat mood swings, and circadian rhythm disorders, by bringing safe bright light to photosensitive regions of the brain. 8-12 minutes a day provides the needed amount of light daily.</p>
<p>Valkee especially relieves symptoms like tiredness, anxiety and reduced cognitive performance typical for &lsquo;winter blues&rsquo;. In clinical trials, 9 of 10 participants suffering from severe symptoms experienced a total relief.</p>
<p>5.</p>
<p><a href="http://visionwiz.net/2010/02/revahealthcom-find-medical-help-and-clinics/">RevHealth</a></p>
<p>RevaHealth.com is a healthcare search engine. It gather the information you want about your health .<br>
They will scan the internet so you don&rsquo;t have to spend hours crawling it yourself.They can help you find clinics for laser eye surgery, cosmetic surgery, dentistry, fertility treatment, hair loss problems, and lots, lots more.</p>
