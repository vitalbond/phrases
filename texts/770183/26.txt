<div><p align="center"><strong>iCitizen</strong>:</p>
<p align="center">iCitizen is a new startup that aims to help you &nbsp;efficiently follow and speak out about issues that mean the most to you.</p>
<p align="center">By using this app you can track the current issues most important to you, and keep up with related news.</p>
<p>&nbsp;With this app you would be able to know more about your elected representatives and have a simple way to share your views &mdash; all from the palm of your hand.</p>
<p align="center"><a href="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/lucky-entrepreneurs.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29942" alt="track elected representative" src="http://visionwiz.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/lucky-entrepreneurs.jpg" width="100" height="90"></a></p>
<p align="center"><strong><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">How It Works:</span></strong></p>
<p align="center"><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">All you need to do is just download its apps on your smartphone and start tracking elected officials and the issues you care about in real time.</span></p>
<p align="center">You can also take part in polls to let your representative know where you stand on hot-button topics.</p>
<p>You can cast your vote in polls related to today&rsquo;s issues and &nbsp;show your support for relevant legislation.</p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">You can &nbsp;also&nbsp;</span>rate your federal and local elected officials. View their voting records and campaign contributors.</p>
