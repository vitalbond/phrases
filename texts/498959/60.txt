<div><p>Hi Friends!</p>
<p>So remember last week when I was <a href="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/15-non-traditional-salads/" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>talking about non-traditional salads</strong></span></a>, and I was saying that hubby can be totally happy eating just a giant bowl of lettuce with a few broccoli stems on top and calling it a salad? Well, in that post I also mentioned that I&rsquo;m not actually the biggest fan of lettuce. Give me ALL THE TOPPINGS though! Who&rsquo;s with me?</p>
<p>Well, if you are with me&hellip;then you&rsquo;ll probably like this Salmon Chop Salad. It&rsquo;s basically lots of goodies that I would top a traditional salad with&hellip;.sans lettuce.</p>

<p>Here&rsquo;s my recipe. Lucky for you, this &ldquo;recipe&rdquo; is about as lax as it gets. <p>Feel free to prepare the veggies however you want. I microwaved my green beans and sweet potatoes but next time I&rsquo;ll definitely grill or roast them! If I could eat feta right now, I definitely would have included it&hellip;but if you don&rsquo;t like it, leave it out or add your personal favorite cheese. Cubes of fresh mozzarella would be great!</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12316" src="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/salmon-chop-salad.jpg.jpg" alt="salmon chop salad.jpg Salmon Chop Salad" width="550" height="368" title="Salmon Chop Salad"></p>
<p>Another good thing about this salad &ndash; you can pretty much top it with any dressing and it will be delicious. So I&rsquo;m leaving that up to you. If it turns out, I&rsquo;ll share it with you guys!</p>
