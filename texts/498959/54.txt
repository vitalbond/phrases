<p>For my post today, I decided to share one of my favorite kinds of recipes: no-bake energy balls! If you&rsquo;ve been following Running on Real Food, you&rsquo;re probably familiar with my love for really, really easy snack recipes. Everyone seems to be busy these days I think we can all use simple, yet nutritious and delicious recipes in our lives. Having healthy snacks on hand can prevent overeating, help you make better food choices, get you through that afternoon slump and provide the energy you need for busy days and tough workouts.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12120" src="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/3-vegan-gingerbread-protein-energy-balls.jpg" alt="3 vegan gingerbread protein energy balls Gingerbread Protein Balls" width="550" height="752" title="Gingerbread Protein Balls"></p>
<p>Today&rsquo;s no-bake energy ball variety features one of my favourite flavours: gingerbread! The molasses and ground ginger give them that classic gingerbread flavour while the cashew butter, protein powder and ground flax provide an excellent nutritional profile. These are backed with fibre, protein, essential fatty acids and they taste just like gingerbread cookie dough!</p>
