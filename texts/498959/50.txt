<p>Time for another quick recipe. I skipped out on food prep this weekend because my parents were in town until Sunday afternoon and by then I was too tired from the weekend to do it. So, this week I&rsquo;ve been throwing stuff together &nbsp;and making it work since we didn&rsquo;t go to the grocery store either.</p>
<p>This dinner comes together in under 20 minutes and requires very few ingredients. I made it even easier on myself by pre-chopping the chicken and veggies before I left for Crossfit so that all I had to do when I got home was dump things in a pan.</p>

&nbsp;
<p>I served mine with sweet potato noodles because I wanted to try out my new spiralizer. I just sauteed them lightly for a few minutes before I started cooking the chicken, set them aside and then added them back in at the very end. This would also be good served over brown rice or quinoa. Don&rsquo;t like bok choy? Choose another favorite veggie like green beans or broccoli!</p>
