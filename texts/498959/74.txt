<p>This recipe is great. Why? Because it can cater to the entire family, kids, adults, and every one in between!</p>
<p>Who said chicken tenders were only for kids?! When you make a delicious, gluten free, healthier version, you&rsquo;re kids will never know, and the adults will be happy to devour.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12364" src="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/RiceKrispieChicken.jpg" alt="RiceKrispieChicken Rice Krispie Chicken Tenders" width="550" height="825" title="Rice Krispie Chicken Tenders"></p>
<p>Rice Krispy Chicken Tenders? Yep! Using brown rice krispies, I made this recipe gluten free.&nbsp; And of course, no frying here. Simply baked for simplicity and to avoid all that unneeded oil and fat.</p>
<p>And what is that dipping sauce? Who loves Chick-fil-A sauce?! Well here is a healthier alternative that tastes great on these tenders!</p>
<p>For children, you can simply serve this chicken tenders recipe with the homemade Chick-fil-A sauce I also am sharing in today&rsquo;s post to them as dinner, or even make a chicken tenders sandwich for their lunch! And for us older folks (yeah, I&rsquo;m an oldie 21 year old now ☺ ), you can serve these delicious chicken tenders on top of a salad, or make a side of veggies to go along with the tenders!</p>
<p>A meal that is simple, healthy, yummy, and can please everyone? That is the kind of recipe I love as a busy college student living on a hectic schedule.</p>
