<p>In case you missed out&nbsp;<strong><a style="color: #03764d;" href="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/pin-it-party-4/" target="_blank">on the last one</a></strong>, here are the rules:</p>
<p>All bloggers are welcome to participate! Here&rsquo;s what you do:</p>
<ul><li>Round up 5 blog posts you&rsquo;ve written that you would like to see on Pinterest.</li>
<li>Make sure there is a nice, pinable image or photo included in each post.</li>
<li>Write a short post featuring the posts you chose- ie, put 5 nice images &amp; links to the posts all in one place, along with a short description of each post.</li>
<li>On Thursday, publish your post, then visit The Bean and add your post to the linkup! (If you&rsquo;re just reading this today and still want to participate, just publish in the next few days&hellip;I&rsquo;ll leave the linkup open for about a week!)</li>
</ul><h3><span style="color: #ff6600;">The only rule is this:</span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #ff6600;">If you add your post to the linkup, please take a few minutes to visit&nbsp;at least three&nbsp;other posts &nbsp;and&nbsp;pin some of their images!</span></h3>
<p>That&rsquo;s it! Hopefully this will be a fun way to get some Pinterest exposure to some of your best posts and to help drive traffic to your blogs.</p>
<p>If you&rsquo;re a reader, you can still join in the fun if you&rsquo;re on Pinterest! Remember that pinning images from blog posts you love is a great way to support your favorite bloggers! It helps more people see the posts and can help the blogs grow! So, if you&rsquo;re a reader in the mood to pin, visit the Pin It Party, check out some of the great posts that are linked up and pin away!</p>
