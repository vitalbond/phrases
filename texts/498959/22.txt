<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Banana Bread recipe</strong></span>: 2 medium ripe bananas (mashed), 2 eggs, 1/4 cup yogurt, 1/4 cup butter (melted), 1/2 cup sugar, 2 cups four, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cinnamon. Mix the bananas, eggs, yogurt, butter and sugar until well combined. Add the flour, baking soda and cinnamon. Pour into a greased loaf pan and bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes.&nbsp;</span></p>
