<div itemprop="description" class="ERSSummary">These gluten-free, savory cheesy quinoa muffins are loaded with fresh tomato and basil for the perfect party appetizer or nutritious snack!</div>
<div class="divERSHeadItems">
<div class="ERSAuthor">Author: <span itemprop="author">Alexis</span></div>
<div class="ERSCategory">Recipe type: <span itemprop="recipeCategory">Appetizer, Snack</span></div>
<div class="ERSServes">Serves: <span itemprop="recipeYield">12</span></div></div>
<div class="ERSIngredients">
<div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">Ingredients</div>
<ul><li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac12; cup quinoa, rinsed</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 cup vegetable broth (or water*)</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2 eggs, beaten</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2 tbsp almond meal</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; cup shredded mozzarella cheese</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; cup grated Parmesan cheese</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2-3 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; tsp salt</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 cup diced tomatoes (about 2 medium Roma tomatoes)</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; cup chopped fresh basil</li>
</ul><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSInstructions">
<div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div>
<ol><li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Preheat oven to 350F. Spray a 12-cup muffin tin liberally with cooking spray. You could also use a mini muffin tin and adjust the cooking time to 20 minutes.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">In a medium pot, bring vegetable broth and quinoa to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for about 15 minutes (or until all the liquid is absorbed).</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Dump cooked quinoa into a bowl and set aside to cool.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">In a large bowl, stir together beaten eggs, almond meal, cheeses, garlic and salt. Add in cooled quinoa and stir to combine. Fold in tomatoes and basil.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Spoon batter evenly into muffin tin. Bake for about 25 minutes. Allow muffins to cool in pan for at least 20 minutes before carefully removing them from tin.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Serve warm.</li>
</ol><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSNotesDiv">
<div class="ERSNotesHeader">Notes</div>
<div class="ERSNotes">*if you use water, you&rsquo;ll need to add more salt to the batter.</div>
