<h3 style="text-align: center;">Leftover banana bread<br>
+ nut&nbsp;butter<br>
+ dark chocolate<br>
=<br>
Banana Bread Truffles.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong><span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0); text-decoration: underline;">Do it.&nbsp;</span></strong></span></h3>
<p style="text-align: left;">Best guess: I used about 2 cups of crumbled banana bread, 2/3 cup of peanut butter and 1/2 a cup of melted dark chocolate chips.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Mix the crumbles and the nut butter&nbsp;together.<br>
Form into balls.<br>
Freeze for 30 minutes.<br>
Dip in melted chocolate.<br>
Store in the freezer.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Use your favorite banana bread recipe and your favorite nut butter. If your bread is drier, you may want to try adding a splash of milk when you&rsquo;re forming the balls.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Enjoy!<br>
