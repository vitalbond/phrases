<div class="ERSIngredients">
<div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">Ingredients</div>
<ul><li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2 cups diced sweet potatoes (1 medium)</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 Tablespoon coconut oil, melted</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac12; teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">cinnamon, to taste</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">Greek yogurt (vanilla or plain)</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">pumpkin seeds, optional</li>
</ul><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSInstructions">
<div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div>
<ol><li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Spread the sweet potatoes out on a foil-lined baking sheet.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Mix melted coconut oil and vanilla in a small dish and pour over potatoes.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Stir until well coated, then sprinkle with cinnamon.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Roast at 425 degrees F for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sweet potatoes reach desired doneness.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Layer sweet potatoes and greek yogurt in a bowl and top with pumpkin seeds.</li>
</ol><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSNotesDiv">
<div class="ERSNotesHeader">Notes</div>
<div class="ERSNotes">Replace the pumpkin seeds with your favorite nut or seed if you&rsquo;d like. <br>To sweeten further, you could add a sprinkle of sugar before roasting or add a drizzle of honey to your parfait.</div>
<div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
