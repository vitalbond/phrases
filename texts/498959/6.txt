<h3 style="text-align: center;">Black Bean Guacamole</h3>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11904" alt="black bean guacamole.jpg {Refreshed} Black Bean Guacamole" src="http://www.theleangreenbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/black-bean-guacamole.jpg.jpg" width="550" height="357" title="{Refreshed} Black Bean Guacamole"></p>
<p>This is basically your average guacamole, just taken to a new level. I added black beans (which you can&rsquo;t even taste) for protein and fiber and also mixed in some Greek yogurt for a protein boost!</p>
<p>Here&rsquo;s how you make it:</p>

<div class="ERSIngredients">
<div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">Ingredients</div>
<ul><li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2 medium avocados</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 c black beans, cooked</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">â…“ c plain Greek yogurt</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">juice from one lemon</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac12; c red onion, diced</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 medium tomato, diced</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 jalapeno, minced</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2-4 Tbsp fresh cilantro, minced</li>
</ul><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSInstructions">
<div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div>
<ol><li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Place the black beans in a small food processor and pulse several times.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add one of the avocados and the lemon juice and pulse until well mixed.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">In a small bowl, smash the remaining avocado with a fork.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Stir in the greek yogurt.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add the black bean mixture, along with the onion, tomato, jalapeno and cilantro.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Mix well and serve with tortilla chips.</li>
</ol><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
