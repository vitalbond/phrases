<div class="ERSIngredients">
<div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">Ingredients</div>
<ul><li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">1 egg</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; teaspoon baking soda</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac12; cup almond meal</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac12; cup plain greek yogurt</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">2 teaspoons vinegar</li>
<li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">&frac14; cup cornmeal</li>
</ul><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSInstructions">
<div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div>
<ol><li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Combine egg, yogurt and vinegar in a small bowl.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Stir in baking soda, almond meal and cornmeal until just combined.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Let sit for 5 minutes while you heat the pan/griddle.</li>
<li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Pour or scoop batter onto griddle, let sit until bubbles form and flip carefully.</li>
</ol><div class="ERSClear"></div></div>
<div class="ERSNotesDiv">
<div class="ERSNotesHeader">Notes</div>
<div class="ERSNotes">Optional: If you want to give these a little more flavor, add in some of your favorite spices!</div>
