<div><div class="field field-type-text field-field-body">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>ICRW and Partners for Prevention will conduct a randomized controlled trial of school-based, violence prevention interventions for young adolescents across three countries in Asia: Vietnam, India and Bangladesh.&nbsp; The intervention in each site is based on the Gender Equity Movement in Schools (GEMS) program that ICRW and partners developed and tested in Mumbai, India, from 2008-2010.&nbsp; That study demonstrated the feasibility and importance of intervening early to shift attitudes and norms around gender equality and gender-based violence.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The current study will make a significant contribution to the evidence base of what works in developing gender-equitable and non-violent attitudes and behaviors&nbsp;among boys and girls through rigorous, mixed-method evaluations across three sites in the region. &nbsp;</p>        </div>
