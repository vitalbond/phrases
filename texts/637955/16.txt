<div class="field field-type-text field-field-duties">
      <div class="field-label">Primary Duties:&nbsp;</div>
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>&nbsp;</p><p>. More specifically, the Candidate will work under the supervision of ICRW to:</p><p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Identify and summarize recent research on barriers to adolescent sexual and reproductive health (ASRH) in urban Dhaka by local organizations and research institutions.</p><p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Assist in design of data collection approach and lead the administration of a household-based survey of approximately 300 married girls &lt; 19 years of age to provide robust evidence on barriers to sexual and reproductive health access to services in two urban slum communities in Dhaka.</p><p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Assist in the study design and development of field guides and conduct a series of qualitative research methods (at least 1 community mapping; 2 focus group discussions; and 30 in-depth interviews) in one urban slum community to assess the: a) healthcare service options and barriers for adolescent girls; and b) emerging ASRH challenges for both married and unmarried adolescent girls ages 15-19.</p><p>We will consider candidates with expertise in only either quantitative or qualitative data collection, but would prefer to identify an organization that can effectively manage both quantitative and qualitative data collection and preparation of data for analysis.&nbsp;</p>        </div>
