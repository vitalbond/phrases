<div class="field field-type-text field-field-body">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>In 2009, ICRW published <em>Girls Speak: A New Voice in Global Development </em>as the fourth report of the <em>Girls Count </em>report series. Four years later, researchers supported by the Nike Foundation asked more than 500 girls from 14 countries to share their insights and perspectives with global decision-makers. This report synthesizes girls&rsquo; voices from around the world and argues that girls&rsquo; insights are crucial to designing effective global development policies. &nbsp;</p>        </div>
