<div class="field field-type-text field-field-bio">
      <div class="field-label">Bio:&nbsp;</div>
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>Shubh Sharma is a Technical Specialist at the International Center for Research on Women (ICRW). In this capacity, she provides management and research support to ICRW&rsquo;s evaluation of Gap Inc.&rsquo;s Personal Advancement and Career Enhancement (P.A.C.E) Program for women factory workers across seven countries in Asia. Prior to joining ICRW, Sharma worked on several evaluations of women&rsquo;s and adolescent girls&rsquo; programs as well as on policy research concerning women&rsquo;s work and education. She has also been in involved in training and networking around feminist evaluation in India.&nbsp;</p>        </div>
