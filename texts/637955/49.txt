<div><div class="field field-type-text field-field-body">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>In India, gender inequality manifests in many different ways, including a preference for sons over daughters.&nbsp; Girl&rsquo;s lack of value and son preference are key drivers of gender-biased sex selection at birth, which is responsible for skewing India&rsquo;s female to male sex ratio in favor of boys.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>This large-scale study examines men&rsquo;s and women&rsquo;s underlying attitudes and behaviors around son preference, with a particular focus on how they conceptualize manhood and masculinity.&nbsp; The study is being carried out&nbsp;in seven Indian states (Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh) involving more than nine thousand men and three thousand women, ages 18 to&nbsp;49.&nbsp; The study looks at what factors influence men&rsquo;s views about masculinity, as measured by&nbsp;their support for gender equality and control of women&rsquo;s behaviour, and how these views about masculinity&nbsp;impact on their preference for sons and perpetration of intimate partner violence. &nbsp;</p>        </div>
