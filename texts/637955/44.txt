                    <p>In April 2013, ICRW produced a critical review of currently collected indicators to measure adolescent sexual and reproductive health (ASRH).&nbsp; The report from this work provided a summary of the current state of knowledge about ASRH in Guatemala and Honduras and recommended a core set of indicators to capture progress toward improved ASRH in the region.&nbsp; ICRW is now exploring how best to build on our recent work to collectively advance the ASRH agenda in Mesoamerica and beyond.&nbsp;</p>        </div>
