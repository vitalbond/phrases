<div><div class="field field-type-text field-field-subtitle">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    Summary of the Evidence        </div>
        </div>
</div>
<div class="field field-type-text field-field-body">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>This policy brief&nbsp;highlights five evidence-based&nbsp;strategies identified by ICRW to delay or prevent child marriage:&nbsp;1) Empower girls with information, skills and support networks; 2) Provide economic support and incentives to girls and their families; 3) Educate and rally parents and community members; 4) Enhance girls' access to a high-quality education; and 5) Encourage supportive laws and policies.&nbsp;In order for the next generation of development programs to make ending child marriage a priority, policymakers must pay attention to these strategies while&nbsp;continuing to test innovative approaches and evaluation techniques.</p>        </div>
