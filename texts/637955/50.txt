<div><div class="field field-type-text field-field-body">
    <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
                    <p>A preference for sons over daughters has a detrimental impact on women's and girls' health and well being.&nbsp; It also affects society by&nbsp;driving gender-biased, sex selection resulting in a skewed ratio&nbsp;of females&nbsp;to males at birth in favor of males.</p>
<p>This report presents the findings of a large-scale study that examines men&rsquo;s and women&rsquo;s underlying attitudes and behaviors around son preference, with a particular focus on how they conceptualize manhood and masculinity.&nbsp; The study was&nbsp;conducted in seven Indian states (Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh) involving more than nine thousand men and three thousand women, aged 18-49.&nbsp; The study&nbsp;identified key&nbsp;factors that influence men&rsquo;s views about masculinity, as measured by their&nbsp;support for gender equality and control of women&rsquo;s behaviour, and how these views impact on their preference for sons and perpetration of intimate partner violence. &nbsp;</p>        </div>
