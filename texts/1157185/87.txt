<p><strong>Choose Your Destination and Trip Distance</strong></p>
<p>Your road trip can be any length, from two hours to two weeks to two months or more. As you decide where you want to travel, consider these factors:</p>
<p>- Weather during your planned visit<br>
- Things to do at your destination<br>
- Museums<br>
- Outdoor activities</p>
<p><strong>Road Trip Costs</strong></p>
<p>As you plan your trip, make a note of the costs connected with each day of travel so that you can stay within your travel budget. Here are some categories to consider:</p>
<p>- Lodging<br>
- Gas<br>
- Food</p>
