<p>Confused about trends for Spring and Summer? You&rsquo;re not alone! Keeping track of everything is indeed overwhelming, and most of us don&rsquo;t have time to flip through magazines on a daily basis. Gen Bell, wardrobe stylist is here to assist!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72606" alt="Gen Bell" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/MG_00372.jpg" width="410" height="568"></p>
<p><strong>What&rsquo;s in and out for Spring in terms of silhouettes and colors?</strong></p>
<p>White, pastels, floral prints, and bright bold colors are definitely in this Spring.</p>
<div></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/g.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72419];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72461" alt="Gen Bell" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/g.png" width="580" height="435"></a></div>
<div>
<div></div>
<div><strong>Are there any items that can be transitioned from Winter to Spring?</strong></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div>No! Pack up those winter clothes, and get ready for Spring. You want lightweight and free-flowing pieces for this Spring/Summer.</div>
</div>
<div>
<div></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div><strong>How big of a role do accessories play in everyday outfits?</strong></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div>They aren&rsquo;t that big of a deal if you have the perfect outfit. However, a little bit of accessories can take your outfit a long way. Less is more, so sometimes all you need is that one good statement piece.</div>
</div>
<div>
<div></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div><strong>Go-to outfits for events like graduation, weddings, etc.&nbsp;</strong></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div>It&rsquo;s all about finding that one great silhouette that compliments your body. My personal favorite dress silhouettes that work great for my body type are the wrap dress and the sheath dress.</div>
</div>
