<div><p>Bored with the everyday beauty tools you use? Have you tried <a href="http://artestilebeauty.com/" target="_blank"><b>ArteStile</b></a> (art-eh-steel-eh) beauty tools yet? The tweezers, nail scissors, manicure kit (trim/file) and eyelash curlers are all 100% Made in Italy from Certified Stainless Steel. The ArteStile Collection includes a <b>patented baked enamel </b>for exceptional in-hand feel.</p>
<p>Plus, all the tools contain <b>NO Nickel, NO Lead, NO Chrome and NO Latex </b>making it perfect for sensitive skin. There are so many designs and colours to choose from, including many finishes such as the Collection (with the patented baked enamel), &nbsp;Soft Touch (with a latex-free rubber), Picasso (painted colors) or Classic (pure stainless steel).</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73424" alt="artestilemothersday-662x450" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/artestilemothersday-662x450.png" width="579" height="393"></p>
<p>ArteStile was founded in 2009 on the premise of creating a beauty line that provides high quality products for both professional and everyday at-home use. Inspired by the essence of Italy, where art and beauty play an essential role in everyday life, ArteStile provides the beauty industry with innovative and luxurious products that are as functional as they are a work of art.</p>
<p>Each ArteStile piece is handcrafted in Italy with the finest grade surgical steel for lasting performance. The tips and blades are hand-filed and perfectly aligned to effectively grab even the smallest hairs.</p>
<p>Liz Gillies (Nickelodeon&rsquo;s <i>Victorious</i>) has also designed her own Collection consisting of <a href="http://artestilebeauty.com/product-category/liz-gillies/" target="_blank">tweezers and manicure kits</a>.</p>
