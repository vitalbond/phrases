<div><p><em>Recently</em>, <em>I&rsquo;ve been all of content and dreaming I have been.&nbsp;</em>The <a href="http://davematthewsband.com/" target="_blank">Dave Matthews Band</a> has started their Summer 2014 tour! The 42-date North American tour kicked off on May 16, and each show includes two sets, both acoustic and electric, for the first time ever.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73696" alt="dreaming tree" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/dreaming-tree.jpg" width="580" height="580"></p>
<p>I&rsquo;ve been a member of the <a class="zem_slink" title="Dave Matthews" href="http://www.davematthewsband.com/" target="_blank" rel="homepage">Dave Matthews</a> Band Warehouse fan club for over 10 years.&nbsp; <em>Recently</em>, I went to the shows in both Dallas and Tulsa. I had VIP pit tickets and stood about front row for both shows.&nbsp; The photos are some I took before the sun went down in Dallas.&nbsp; So incredible!</p>
<p>I have also been a huge fan of Dave&rsquo;s Dreaming Tree <a class="zem_slink" title="Wine" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wine" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">wine</a>.&nbsp; Yes, originally I purchased it only because it was Dave&rsquo;s wine, but now it&rsquo;s my go-to choice. &nbsp; A nice of glass of Dreaming Tree <a class="zem_slink" title="Chardonnay" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chardonnay" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Chardonnay</a> in the evening, DMB playing on my iPod, friends and family.&nbsp; It doesn&rsquo;t get much better than that!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73697" alt="Dreaming Tree Wines" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DMB-1.png" width="580" height="580"></p>
<p>The Dreaming Tree wines, a collection of California-based wines from world-renowned musician, Dave Matthews, and award-winning winemaker, Steve Reeder. Concert goers can to purchase The Dreaming Tree wines at select venue locations and sip favorites including &ldquo;Everyday&rdquo; and &ldquo;Crush,&rdquo; aptly named for Dave Matthews Band&rsquo;s hit songs.</p>
<p><iframe src="//www.youtube.com/embed/UWVII0Tgn6M?list=PL25CD7C1AE2A6528D" height="326" width="580" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p>Whether you&rsquo;ve already purchased summer tour tickets, or prefer to listen to DMB&rsquo;s classics at your own backyard barbecue, I hope you will celebrate summer and the Dave Matthews Band by popping open a bottle of The Dreaming Tree wines and toasting to longer days and warmer nights.</p>
<p><iframe src="//www.youtube.com/embed/d-QXbrukN0w?rel=0" height="435" width="580" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73698" alt="Dreaming Tree Wines" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/DMB-2.png" width="580" height="421"></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">We hope you get a chance to catch a show during their summer tour!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73811" alt="Portfolio" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Portfolio-971x1024.jpg" width="579" height="610"></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Available nationwide for around $14.99 a bottle.</p>
