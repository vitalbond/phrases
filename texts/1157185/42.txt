<div><p>Most&nbsp;<a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2014/02/tuesday-trends-fashionable-spring-shoes/">women love sexy shoes</a>, but &ldquo;sexy&rdquo; isn&rsquo;t a one-size-fits-all kind of thing, and just like beauty, it too is in the eye of the beholder. In other words, my kind of shoe may not be your cup of tea and vice versa.</p>
<p><i>Maybe that&rsquo;s why they invented flip-flops?</i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73190" alt="Cute Sandals" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/dreamstime_13382764.jpg" width="580" height="412"></p>
<p>Sexy can be anything from a sleek and slim stiletto, to a thick and chunky fat sole, or even a platform or ballet slipper. Some women like straps, while others prefer a slip-on. Whatever you do, don&rsquo;t get me started on how much I adore peep-toe pumps any time of year or&hellip; how I absolutely abhor those who wear socks [GASP] with sandals.</p>
<p>Who does that?</p>
<p>Why?</p>
<p>Seriously?</p>
<p>Free those toes!</p>
<p>With winter having left the building, it&rsquo;s time to lovingly put away our favorite boots and&nbsp;<a href="http://www.wikihow.com/Pamper-Your-Feet">treat our feet to some much needed loving care</a>&nbsp;before their&nbsp;annual debut in spring and summer &lsquo;sexy&rsquo; &ndash; whatever that may mean to you.</p>
<p>We all have our own style. Sometimes we have multiple styles, dependent on the season [or our mood], but the spring and summer months can bring out the Bohemian babe in all of us &mdash; me included, especially when it comes to shoes.</p>
<p>In warm weather, we tend to want to be outdoors, so for all of those free-spirited romps at local concerts in the park, theme fairs and other outdoor events, finding a great pair of&nbsp;<a href="http://www.dnafootwear.com/women/BedStu">festival sandals</a> is in order.&nbsp;Yes, they need to be comfortable, but come on, we&rsquo;re chics &ndash; we like&rsquo;em cute too!</p>
<p><i>Why pretend otherwise?</i></p>
<p>Even in ancient Greece and Rome, women wore cute sandals. In fact, many of today&rsquo;s sandals look quite similar to those from the days of yore.</p>
<p>And, men? Meh, they&rsquo;ll put on just about anything, unless it&rsquo;s pink. But hey guys? In case you&rsquo;re reading, here&rsquo;s a great <a href="http://www.esquire.com/blogs/mens-fashion/summer-footcare-guide-0513">cheat sheet to help get your feet up to par</a> with the ladies, before you slip on some sandals&hellip; even if they are just for beach wear.</p>
<p>When choosing warm weather footwear, consider more durable materials like leather and hemp so that your purchases are less disposable, and you can avoid any &ldquo;oops, my strap broke&rdquo; moments at the County Fair.</p>
<p>Will you be traipsing around beaches or river beds or just strolling along poolside?&nbsp;What do you need your shoes to do besides look cute and feel comfortable?</p>
<p>These are good questions to consider before you buy your next pair.</p>
<p>There are so many options out there that you can not only accommodate <em>cute</em>, you can also easily address <em>comfort</em> and <em>purpose</em> all in the same pair of shoes.</p>
