<div><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Wear It Wednesday: Hot Yoga Series</strong><br><em>The Athletic Edge Style</em></p>
<div><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72138" alt="yoga" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/yoga.jpg" width="579" height="565"></div>
<p>Hot Yoga is a great way to combine exercise and time with your girlfriends. A weekly hot yoga date with friends gets you out of the house, away from work or kids (or both), and allows you to sweat out your worries.</p>
<p>The fun doesn&rsquo;t have to end after the 60 to 90 minute session; you can replenish your body with a trip to your local health cafe. Or, if you have a planned engagement after your session, where you have to be casually presentable, mimicking the athletic edge style can keep you comfortable and chic.</p>
<p>A loose fitting tee or tank allows for your skin to breathe. A slacked pant (like the trendy harem) can fit over shorts if needed, but it mainly acts in place of jeans for comfort. The wedged sneaker&nbsp;&nbsp;plays to the athletic look and is a great way to pump up your day-to-day outfit.</p>
<p>Finally, a trendy sports bra gives you support, both physically and fashionably.</p>
