<div><p>After wearing slouchy sweaters and jeans all winter, it&rsquo;s nice to finally be able to wear dresses and skirts.&nbsp; I tend to overdo it a little with dresses over spring and summer, but hey, I only get a few months to wear them, so why not? I just love the look. Most can be either worn casually or dressed up with a belt, blazer, heels and jewelry.</p>
<p>Give these trendy dress styles a try this spring:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Vintage</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72002" alt="Spring Forward Dresses" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/vintage2.jpg" width="580" height="870"></p>
<p>Vintage dresses are the perfect choice for a sunny spring day. The flair dresses look flattering on just about any body type.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Maxi</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Maxi.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72000];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72006 aligncenter" alt="Spring Forward Dresses" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Maxi.jpg" width="580" height="889"></a></p>
<p>Maxi dresses are perfect for spring, because the air is still cool. Pair with your favorite flip flops and jacket for a stylish, casual look.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Chiffon</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/chiffon.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72000];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72013 aligncenter" alt="Spring Forward Dresses" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/chiffon.jpg" width="580" height="870"></a></p>
<p>Chiffon dresses are in style this spring. I love the sleeves on this blue one. These are great to wear to weddings or other dressy events.</p>
