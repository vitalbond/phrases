<p>We all need a little rescuing after this long, cold winter!&nbsp; What better way to pamper yourself than to set up your own at-home spa.&nbsp; Indulge in a wonderful scrub or soak, plus some added love for the rest of your body. With only a few special ingredients, you can treat yourself to a relaxing experience without breaking the bank at a local spa. Enjoy this home winter rescue guide!</p>
<p><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">RESCUE ME SCRUB</span></b></p>
<p><b><i>Benefits:</i></b> Exfoliation of dry surface skin and increased hydration</p>
<p><b><i>Ingredients:</i></b><br>
&frac12; cup mineral salts<br>
2 tbsp of any carrier oil such as avocado, jojoba, grapeseed or kukui<br>
5 drops lemongrass oil</p>
<p><b><i>Directions:</i></b><br>
In bowl using a metal spoon, blend mineral salt with carrier oil &ndash; thoroughly mix until it becomes a paste.<br>
Gradually add in lemongrass oil, &nbsp;blending after each drop.<br>
If scrub is too thick, add a few drops of water and re-mix.<br>
Moisten the body in a warm bath or shower, and apply scrub to body by hand or by using a moist loofah pad in circular motions. Using a loofah pad or other textured body sponge or brush will increase the effects of the exfoliation. Beware of being too energetic, as you may scratch the skin. Skin will be rosy and feel energized.<br>
Thoroughly rinse skin and apply body lotion.</p>
<p><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">RESCUE SOAK</span></b></p>
<p><b><i>Benefits:</i></b> Relaxation, detoxification and mineralization of body</p>
<p><b><i>Ingredients:</i></b><br>
4 oz mineral salts<br>
1 cup baking soda</p>
<p><b><i>Directions:</i></b><br>
Add mineral salts to bath while warm water is running. &nbsp;Caution should be taken with hot water, as it may cause lightheadedness.<br>
Add baking soda.<br>
Using a bristle brush, loofah or sisal mitt, dry brush the body in upward strokes &ndash; this will exfoliate the skin, make the treatment more effective and increase blood circulation at the skin&rsquo;s surface, which is key to skin regeneration.<br>
Lower lights and burn a candle at the edge of the tub.&nbsp; Have a glass of cool mineral water and lemon slices nearby to refresh and rehydrate your body. Play soothing, calming music.<br>
Soak at least 15 minutes adding warm water as needed&nbsp;&ndash; the longer the soak, the greater the benefits.<br>
Rinsing is optional &ndash; towel dry and apply body lotion.<br>
Optional &ndash; Add in drops of lemongrass oil to increase aromatic benefits, provide pain relief and soften skin.</p>
<p><b><i>While enjoying your soak, try on these complementary experiences:</i></b><br>
*<a title="Saturday Shopping: From the Lab&rsquo;s Hair Mask No. 718" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2013/11/saturday-shopping-from-the-labs-hair-mask-no-718/">Hair mask</a> &ndash; Massage in a hydrating oil or cream into hair and wrap in shower cap.<br>
*<a title="Makeup Monday: Winter Skin Savers" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2014/03/makeup-monday-winter-skin-savers/">Face treatment</a> &ndash; Apply a hydrating mask or clay mask to the face with a small amount of water.<br>
*<a title="How To Properly Apply Eye Cream" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2013/10/how-to-properly-apply-eye-cream/">Eye treatment</a> &ndash; Boil a chamomile tea bag, and chill and moisten 2 cotton eye pads. Apply to eyes, and place a sleep mask over eyes to secure &ndash; this treatment will not only reduce puffiness and dark circles, but will enhance relaxation benefits due to the exclusion of light and vision.</p>
<p><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">RESCUE MY FEET </span></b></p>
<p><b><i>Benefits:</i></b>&nbsp; Soft, touchable feet</p>
<p><b><i>Ingredients: </i></b><br>
2 tsps mineral salts</p>
<p><b><i>Directions:</i></b><br>
Soak feet in warm water with mineral salts.<br>
Massage scrub (see recipe above) onto feet, and wrap in warm wet towels, allowing towels to cool off.</p>
<p>To warm towels &ndash; put under very hot faucet /squeeze out excess water. Place in microwave unrolled for 1 minute on high (or more time if needed).</p>
<p>Using wet towels, remove scrub.<br>
Use pumice stone or other callous reducer on any tough, thick skin &ndash; be careful not to overdo it or over-sensitize your feet.<br>
Rinse off.<br>
Apply body lotion, and place feet in plastic bags and cotton socks. Follow towel recipe to warm the socks but use a lower microwave setting and less time for maximum benefit. Leave socks on overnight.</p>
<p><b><i>While enjoying your feet treatment, try on these complementary experiences:</i></b><br>
*Warm a hand towel, and place it around your neck to increase relaxation.<br>
*Apply scrub to hands, thoroughly rinse, apply a body lotion, and tuck hands into cotton gloves or mitts for the night.</p>
