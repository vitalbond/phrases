<p style="text-align: left;">Red Carpet Manicure, the world&rsquo;s first at-home LED gel manicure system, is now offering a nail lacquer line! Chic Galleria is a HUGE fan of Red Carpet Manicure, and we&rsquo;ve written about it before:&nbsp;<a title="Finally a Gel Manicure System I Can Use at Home!" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2013/04/finally-gel-manicure-system-home/">Finally a Gel Manicure System I Can Use at Home!</a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;The Red Carpet Manicure Nail Lacquer line launched nationwide at ULTA Beauty, Ulta.com and <a href="http://redcarpetmanicure.com/" target="_blank">redcarpetmanicure.com</a>. This new collection marks the brand&rsquo;s nail lacquer debut and expands Red Carpet Manicure&rsquo;s product line beyond its foundation in the at-home LED gel polish category. The collection includes 52 color shades, and a professional quality base and top coat, each sold separately for $7.99.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The collection&rsquo;s shades range from whites and nudes to a variety of pinks, corals and blues.&nbsp; Additionally, 18 shades in the collection are color-matched to Red Carpet Manicure&rsquo;s LED gel polish line.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Red Carpet Manicure launched in September 2011 as the world&rsquo;s first at-home professional salon LED light activated gel polish system and has since expanded into the nail care category with hand and nail treatments, nail art kits and nail lacquer. Today,&nbsp;Red Carpet Manicure is a trusted brand for all nail care needs. The&nbsp;RCM&nbsp;LED Gel Polish Pro Light Kit retails at $79.99 and the&nbsp;RCM&nbsp;LED Gel Polish Portable Light Starter Kit at $64.99; additional LED shades can be purchased for $9.99.&nbsp;RCM nail lacquer polishes can be purchased for $7.99 each.</p>
