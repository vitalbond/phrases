<div><p>It&rsquo;s a hamper, a trunk, a basket&hellip; whatever you choose to call it! It&rsquo;s time for a summer picnic, and we&rsquo;ve found the perfect combinations of treats from <a href="http://www.harrods.com/content/misc/boutiques/all-year-hampers/" target="_blank">Harrods</a> of London. Who wouldn&rsquo;t enjoy some tea and nibbles directly from Great Britain?</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73832" alt="harrods(1)" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/harrods1.png" width="580" height="580"></p>
<p>You can enjoy the Cartwright &amp; Butler, The Helmsley, s<span style="line-height: 1.5em;">tarting at &pound;50</span><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">. Named after the quaint market town of Helmsley in Yorkshire, this Cartwright and Butler hamper is nostalgic in feel. English Breakfast tea is, of course, mandatory, as are the fruit-filled raspberry preserve and marmalade. In addition, your mouth will thank you for the stem ginger biscuits, Gouda cheese melts and layered chocolate wafer crispies.</span></p>
<p>Or, you can start your day out right with the Breakfast Tray <a href="http://www.harrods.com/content/misc/boutiques/all-year-hampers/" target="_blank">hamper from Harrods</a> for&nbsp;&pound;60. Tea, marmalade and butter crunch biscuits are just a few of the delicious items included. Presented on a wooden breakfast tray, the selection is a true delight that anyone would love to wake up to.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73814" alt="luxury hampers" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/red-case.jpg" width="580" height="349"></p>
<p>My personal favorite is the London Icons Case from <a href="http://www.harrods.com/content/misc/boutiques/all-year-hampers/" target="_blank">Harrods</a>. Full to the brim with a whole host of patriotically-packaged treats, this charming red suitcase will ensure you experience the best of British. Buttery biscuits, fruity preserves, traditional candy and afternoon tea are all included.&nbsp; I LOVE IT!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73815" alt="luxury hampers" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/grande.jpg" width="580" height="350"></p>
<p>If you have a larger company to host, I recommend the&nbsp;Grandeur Food Hall Collection from <a href="http://www.harrods.com/content/misc/boutiques/all-year-hampers/" target="_blank">Harrods</a> for &pound;200.00. This grouping offers an array of fine fares from each of the rooms within our iconic Food Halls. A selection of red and white wines, fine teas, sweet and savory biscuits, preserves and indulgent chocolates are all included.</p>
<p>Now, if the goal of your picnic is romance, Harrods has several tasty hampers to choose from. I&rsquo;d choose the Wedding Hamper from Harrods (even if you aren&rsquo;t married), because inside are Prosecco, sea salt caramel truffles and a heart-shaped candle, which is a perfect combination for couples.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.harrods.com/content/misc/boutiques/all-year-hampers/" target="_blank">Harrods&rsquo; hampers</a> cater to baby showers, bridal showers, birthdays, and much more. A luxury gift for any occasion! Why not pamper yourself too?</p>
