<div><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Himalayan Restaurant -&nbsp;Taste of Nepal (Northern India)</strong></p>
<p>Authentic&nbsp;Indian Spices -&nbsp;Coriander (Cilantro) is a herb with a fresh, sweet aroma. The Coriander powder is an essential&nbsp;spice&nbsp;in the&nbsp;Indian&nbsp;and Nepali cuisine.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Indian-Spices.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72643];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72645" alt="Northern India" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Indian-Spices.jpg" width="580" height="580"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong></strong>Delicious!</p>
<p>The <strong>Himalayan Restaurant</strong> is a charming restaurant in Windsor, CA. An excellent dinning experience all around. From the second you walk in the door, you&rsquo;re greeted with the most pleasing aroma that only a restaurant serving deliciously ethnic food can supply.&nbsp;This restaurant has excellent curries, masalas, and naan.&nbsp;My favorite dishes are the LAMB TIKKA MARSALA AND VEGETABLE SAMOSA &nbsp;(Two crispy pastries stuffed with potato, corn and green peas. Served with a cilantro-mint tamarind chutney&hellip;YUM! (VEGAN)).</p>
