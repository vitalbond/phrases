<p><a href="http://affinitasintimates.com/our-lines/affinitas-ss-2014/" target="_blank">Affinitas</a> makes it her mission to bring beautiful lingerie to women around the world seeking sophisticated &#38; quality lingerie at affordable prices in stylish, quality intimates. To achieve this goal, each piece of Affinitas lingerie is crafted with creative, fashion forward designs, fine, soft-to-the-touch materials, and a special attention to superior comfort and fit.</p>
<p>The brand’s wide selection of bras, panties, camisoles, babydolls, chemises, and bustiers range from basic, everyday styles to special occasion pieces – meaning you can always find the right lingerie for you. Affinitas pieces are made from comfy, luxurious materials hand-picked from around the globe, including silky smooth microfiber, soft fine cotton, high-quality satin and imported French embroidered laces. Sexy trimmings, flirty bows and delicate embellishments are carefully placed to complete each look. Affinitas bras and bustiers span a size range of 30-38 A-DD, with all other items sized XS-XL.</p>
<p><a href="http://affinitasintimates.com/our-lines/parfait-ss-2014/" target="_blank">Parfait</a> by Affinitas is a complementary line to Affinitas Intimates, is distinctively designed to serve the full-busted woman. Parfait bras, bustiers and babydolls are sized 30D – 40G, with coordinating bottoms sized XS – 2X. Developed with fresh, feminine designs and flattering silhouettes, Parfait gives special attention to exceptional support, comfort, and fit. Featuring a wide range of styles from everyday wear to special occasion, Parfait’s bras and bottoms are offered in a color palette from soft and feminine to vibrant and sexy. Stretch satin and lace, soft mesh, textured microfiber, and cotton styles are complimented by satin, lace and tassel trims, bow and charm accents, and intricate embroidery.</p>
