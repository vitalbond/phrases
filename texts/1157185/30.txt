<div><p>You just can&rsquo;t go without skirts this spring, especially after a long and brutally cold winter.</p>
<p>Enjoy the nice weather in these stylish skirts!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Florals</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/floralskirtcollage1.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71854];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72028 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Skirts" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/floralskirtcollage1.jpg" width="580" height="737"></a></p>
<p>There&rsquo;s no doubt about it, florals are a must-have in spring. Floral skirts look fantastic with a dress or casual shirt. Pair with wedges or sandals for a trendy spring look.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Print Maxi</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/maxicollage.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71854];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72030 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Skirts" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/maxicollage.jpg" width="580" height="415"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Wearing a maxi skirt is a stylish way to cover up those legs on a chilly spring day while still dressing up. They come in so many prints, from patterned black to colorful stripes.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Colorful Midi</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/colorfulmidicollage.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71854];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72031 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Skirts" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/colorfulmidicollage.jpg" width="580" height="608"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Midi skirts are a fashionable choice to wear to work. At knee length, they have a professional feel and look incredible with a blouse and heels.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Tulle</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/tullecollage.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71854];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72032 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Skirts" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/tullecollage.jpg" width="580" height="438"></a></p>
<p>Tulle skirts are gorgeous. They are dressy and look amazing when worn with accessories such as jewelry, belts or purses. These skirts are both eye-catching and fun!</p>
