<p>While the weather is always pleasant in the Bay Area, springtime is the best time to get out and experience the City by the Bay. Here&rsquo;s a list of places to visit and things to do!</p>
<p><strong> San Francisco Giants Game</strong></p>
<p>By now, the season is in full swing. With two National Championships under their belt, the Giants are a must-see team. Aim to go to a weekend afternoon game (night games can be a bit chilly). Not a fan of baseball? You can still dress up and admire the view! If all else fails, you can always walk down Embarcadero!</p>
<p><strong>Mission Dolores Park</strong></p>
<p>Right in the heart of the Mission District, Dolores Park boasts the best views of the city in tandem with a relaxing day in the park. Located near Valencia Street and the Castro, Dolores Park offers a plethora of things to do in the surrounding area. You can even bring your children, as the playground is an adventure in itself.</p>
<p><strong>The Japanese Tea Garden</strong></p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">Located in Golden Gate Park,</span><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">&nbsp;</span>The Japanese Tea Garden is always great to visit, but it&rsquo;s best in the Spring when the cherry blossoms are in bloom! You can spend the day walking in the sun, and don&rsquo;t forget to take a few pictures at the&nbsp;Hagiwara Gate!</p>
