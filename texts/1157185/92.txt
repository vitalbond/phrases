<p><b>Le Vin </b>is a crafted-in-California <b>collection of candles and diffusers in seven luxurious scents that speak to the abundant bouquet of fine wines and memorable vineyards</b>. Celebrating wine country living with naturally graceful style, this is the first home fragrance collection from OLAVIE, recognized as an exceptional spa resource for its Chardonnay-based skincare, favored by five star resorts in luxury destinations, such as Aspen. Available online at <a href="http://www.olavie.com/" target="_blank">www.olavie.com</a>, the candles are priced at $34, and the diffusers are $45.</p>
<p>Each of the seven scents – Tuscan Fig, Sparkling Violet, Cassis, French Oak, White Grapefruit, Vineyard Garden and the authentically masculine, Signature Cellar – are meant to inspire an armchair adventure – a virtual journey to wine country, California and beyond.  As each essence evolves, the experience brings to mind a day among the vines, at first, shrouded in cool, mysterious fog; then, as the atmosphere warms, these scents become brighter, sparking in sunlight, and finally, as the layers of these vineyard aromas become deeper and richer, they take on a romantic note, connecting earth, sun and sky.</p>
