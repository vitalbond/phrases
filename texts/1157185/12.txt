<div><p><b>Art + Science</b>, a full-service brand licensing company debuts <b>Bitty Bettys&trade;, </b>a hot new line of fragrances sold at <b>Hot</b> <b>Topic </b>stores nationwide. The perfume line is composed of four varieties, <i>Betty, Rita, Liberty, </i>and <i>Cindy</i>.&nbsp; Each Bitty Betty has her own distinct style, personality, and fragrance profile.&nbsp; In addition, for the first time ever, consumers can touch the box to release the scent before they purchase.&nbsp; This is good news for retailers too!&nbsp; This unique &ldquo;try me&rdquo; feature is made possible by <b><i>Celessence&trade; Technologies</i></b>, a microencapsulation delivery system that allows for effective scent infusion in print and textiles.&nbsp; With a light touch, consumers can sample fragrances without cumbersome testers.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72191" alt="Bitty Bettys" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/betty.jpg" width="580" height="397"></p>
<p><b>Betty</b>: Betty is flirty and fun!&nbsp; She is always sure to have her red lipstick and a dirty mouth to bring a little fun to the party.&nbsp; Betty&rsquo;s scent is composed of orange citrus and white tea-leaves with hints of jasmine, rose petals and musk.</p>
<p><b>Rita</b>: A fierce warrior, with fiery hair to match, Rita is always boosting the morale of others, and is a master of the baton act!&nbsp; Rita&rsquo;s scent is notably Italian bergamot with the subtle aromas of rose, violet, and wood.</p>
<p><b>Liberty</b>:&nbsp; Liberty is a classic beauty that sails the high seas with a smile. &nbsp;Liberty&rsquo;s scent is composed mainly of Mediterranean and citrus with subtle hints of florals throughout the scent profile.</p>
<p><b>Cindy</b>: Cindy is a true cowgirl who enjoys square dancing, BBQ and rolling in the hay!&nbsp; Cindy&rsquo;s scent is primarily citrus, including passion fruit and berries with undertones that include magnolia, freesia, cedar wood and amber.</p>
<p>&ldquo;Bringing the technology of <b><i>Celessence&trade;</i></b> to the <b>Bitty Bettys&trade;</b> fragrance line is a truly innovative approach to the way consumers can sample a fragrance product before purchasing it,&rdquo; says Stone Newman, CEO and Founder of <b>Art + Science<i>.&nbsp; </i></b>&ldquo;Use of Celessence encapsulation technology will result in products that engage consumers from touch to smell, thereby extending the consumer experience.&rdquo;</p>
<p><b><i>Celessence<sup>TM</sup> Technologies&rsquo;</i></b> innovative microencapsulation technology uses touch, friction or motion to release and deliver the benefits of scent, freshness or skincare actives.&nbsp;&nbsp; Microcapsules are actually bound to a surface with a proprietary binder that ensures controlled release that is long lasting, affordable and even wash-durable!</p>
