<div><p>Gala Bingo believes that enjoying life with friends and participating in a more social life can have a positive impact on your health and your standard of living. The &lsquo;<a href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-23045905" target="_blank">The social brain theory</a>&rsquo; suggests that animals in large social groups have bigger brains and that sophisticated communication is required. Theorists also believe that this directly translates to humans with <a href="http://www.slate.com/articles/health_and_science/human_evolution/2012/10/human_brain_size_social_groups_led_to_the_evolution_of_large_brains.html" target="_blank">Oxford&rsquo;s Robin Dunbar</a> arguing that the evolution of human brain was driven by our increasingly complex social relationships.</p>

With 137 local clubs, Gala Bingo is a great, local place to meet new people as well as socialize with friends and family.</p>
