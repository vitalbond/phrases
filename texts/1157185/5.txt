<div><p>Spring is almost here, which means a new set of trends! It&rsquo;s easy to feel overwhelmed trying to follow what&rsquo;s in and what&rsquo;s out. So the best way to begin is by focusing on jewelry! While not as salient as clothing, jewelry ties an outfit together and makes a statement on its own. Here are three of my favorite Spring trends and where to find pieces to add to your collection!</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/CRYSTSL.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71090];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-71091" alt="Spring Jewelry Trends" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/CRYSTSL.png" width="519" height="146"></a>Add these translucent beauties to your wardrobe for an elegant feel. No longer reserved solely for special occasions, crystals are delicate in nature but incredibly alluring.</p>
<p><a href="http://lulufrost.com/">Lulu Frost</a> provides a collection of <a href="http://lulufrost.com/collections/classics">classic pieces</a> that are sure to add character to your wardrobe.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/k.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71090];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-71094" alt="Spring Jewelry Trends" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/k.png" width="518" height="174"></a></p>
<p>Make your transition into spring a bold one with <a href="http://bit.ly/1dRDhtS">Baublebar</a>. Known for its fashion jewelry, the store has an excellent <a href="http://bit.ly/1eE8ouk" target="_blank">inventory</a>&nbsp;of boldly colored necklaces, earrings and bracelets. Fresh mint, peach and purple tones create the perfect&nbsp;<a title="LIVE STREAM: MBFW NY SPRING 2014 COLLECTIONS" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2013/09/live-stream-mbfw-ny-spring-2014-collections/">spring look</a>!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Untitled-16.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-71090];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter" alt="Spring Jewelry Trends" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Untitled-16.png" width="516" height="244"></a></p>
<p>The chain necklace is the perfect way to dress up a casual outfit. Since it comes in a variety of colors and types, you&rsquo;re sure to find one that fits your style.</p>
<p><a href="http://bit.ly/K40gdF" target="_blank">Gorjana Griffin</a>, in addition to their already gorgeous collection,&nbsp;offers a&nbsp;<a href="http://bit.ly/1dRDXzo" target="_blank">Bristol Link Necklace</a>&nbsp;which is a modern twist on the standard chain link.</p>
