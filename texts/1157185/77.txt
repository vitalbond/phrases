<div><p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73396" alt="IMG_0607" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0607.jpg" width="580" height="870"></p>
<p>Who&nbsp;doesn&rsquo;t&nbsp;like <a class="zem_slink" title="Crab Cakes" href="http://www.mccormick.com/Recipes/Main-Dishes/Classic-OLD-BAY-Crab-Cakes" target="_blank" rel="mccormick">Crab Cakes</a>?&nbsp;My version has a&nbsp;spicy kick&nbsp;which is complimented by the tangy&nbsp;citrus sour cream-based sauce. Sear the&nbsp;cakes&nbsp;on both sides until nicely browned and line them up on the baking sheet.&nbsp;Once your party starts with the&nbsp;hors d&rsquo;oeuvres,&nbsp;pop the cakes into the oven for 8 more minutes to just cook through and serve warm at the table with greens dressed in simple vinaigrette, sauce and lemon slices.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73387" alt="IMG_0538" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0538.jpg" width="579" height="868"></p>
<p>Ingredients: jumbo lump crab meat,&nbsp;red onion,&nbsp;bread crumbs,&nbsp;Dijon mustard,&nbsp;mayonnaise,&nbsp;egg,&nbsp;lemon,&nbsp;dill,&nbsp;red pepper flakes, sour cream,&nbsp;chives,&nbsp;lime,&nbsp;mixed greens, white wine vinegar, extra-virgin olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73388" alt="IMG_0539" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0539.jpg" width="580" height="870"></p>
<p>Jumbo Lump Crab meat is sweet, moist and delicious. Go for a good stuff, I bought mine freshly packaged from&nbsp;Citarella&rsquo;s fish section. When handling the meat, break the pieces ever so gently to ensure that large lumps are retained.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73389" alt="IMG_0542" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0542.jpg" width="580" height="869"></p>
<p>Gently break up the crab meat with your fingers but make ensure that the large lumps and flakes are retained.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73390" alt="IMG_0558" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0558.jpg" width="579" height="869"></p>
<p>In a medium mixing bowl, combine <span class="span">crab meat</span>, <span class="span">mustard</span>, <span class="span">mayonnaise</span>, <span class="span">egg</span>, <span class="span">lemon juice</span>, <span class="span">dill</span>, <span class="span">red pepper flakes</span>, <span class="span">bread crumbs</span>, <span class="span">sea salt</span> and <span class="span">pepper</span>.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73391" alt="IMG_0561" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0561.jpg" width="580" height="870"></p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5;">Then add <span class="span">saut&eacute;ed onion</span>. Gently mix everything well with your hands. The mixture should not be mushy. Cover with the plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour or more to let the flavors blend and the ingredients to bind well together. <span class="span">Kitchen tip:</span>&nbsp;To check for seasoning, make a small test patty and fry it on the pan, try it and adjust the seasoning if needed.</span></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73392" alt="IMG_0563" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0563.jpg" width="580" height="871"></p>
<p>Shape the crab mixture into 12 cakes of about 2-inches in diameter each.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73393" alt="IMG_0570" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0570.jpg" width="579" height="869"></p>
<p>In a non-stick pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add 6 cakes at a time to the pan and cook for 3 minutes on each side, then flip and cook for another 3 minutes until nicely browned.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73394" alt="IMG_0576" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0576.jpg" width="579" height="386"></p>
<p>Transfer the cakes to a baking sheet. Bake for 8 minutes.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73395" alt="IMG_0588" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_0588.jpg" width="579" height="869"></p>
<p>Make the sauce: Whisk together sour cream and lime juice, season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with chopped chives.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73386" alt="crab cakes 1" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/crab-cakes-1.jpg" width="580" height="870"></p>
<p>Serve the crab cakes warm with lemon wedges, sauce and a simple salad.</p>
