<p><a href="http://www.vdhent.com/" target="_blank"><strong>van der Hagen Enterprises</strong></a> is the premier specialty bar soap manufacturer in the world. The van der Hagen’s have supplied soaps to many of the most respected companies in the personal care industry, including: Avon, Harrods (UK), Bath &#38; Body Works, Liz Claiborne, The Body Shop (UK), Michael Jordan, Boots (UK), O’Boticario (Brazil), Elizabeth Arden, Walt Disney, Estée Lauder, and Warner Bros., to name a few.</p>
<p><strong>van der Hagen Enterprises</strong> owes its renowned reputation as producers of the finest quality soaps to their use of the old-fashioned kettle process. During this process, carefully selected ingredients are added to a heated kettle by a master compounder and according to exacting temperatures. Once complete, the thin liquid is poured into molds, run through a cooling tunnel and hand packed. The result? A unique formula which contains over 40% humectant moisturizers. In addition, the <a href="http://www.vdhent.com/products/premium.html" target="_blank">Deluxe Shave Soap</a> contains Aloe Vera &#38; Shea Butter, and the Glycerin Shave Soap contains Vitamin E &#38; Oat Extract.</p>
<p>The kettle process is more labor-intensive and the finer ingredients more expensive, but the results speak for themselves. van der Hagen shave soaps not only provide for the closest shave, they also are hypo-allergenic and rinse more completely than typical soaps, leaving your skin feeling soft and smooth.</p>
