<div><p>Now that we&rsquo;re finally out of the winter slum, it&rsquo;s time to be a little more daring with our wardrobes.</p>
<p>Make your outfit stand out with these fun spring accessories:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Big Hats</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/hatscollage.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72004];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72037 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Accessories" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/hatscollage.jpg" width="580" height="484"></a></p>
<p>Hats can make or break an outfit. Worn the proper way, they can really add to what may otherwise be a plain look. They especially look great with long hair.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bold Jewelry</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/statementjewelrycollage.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72004];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72040 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Accessories" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/statementjewelrycollage.jpg" width="580" height="654"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Statement jewelry are definitely making statements this spring. Go bold with colorful and eye-catching necklaces, bracelets and rings.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bright Purses</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/purses.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72004];player=img;"><img class="size-full wp-image-72144 aligncenter" style="border: 1px solid black;" alt="Spring Accessories" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/purses.jpg" width="580" height="649"></a></p>
<p>Spring purses add radiant pops of color to an outfit. Wear a brightly colored purse with a complementary-colored outfit for a striking spring look.</p>
