<div><p>Designer <b>Lisa LeCroy (<a href="http://www.lisalecroy.com/" target="_blank">www.lisalecroy.com</a>) </b>has created a convertible shoe and accessory collection to help women get more out of their boots. Lisa LeCroy Boots are made with soft suedes, smooth nappa leathers, silky Italian hair-calf, sturdy canvas, Spanish Aztec fabric, plus, leathers of the highest quality. With an assortment of completely interchangeable uppers with stirrups and buttons to choose from, you can easily convert your bootie into a lace-up riding boot.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-71964" alt="Lisa LeCroy" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DP2B6612c-819x1024.jpg" width="580" height="726"></p>
<p>Clara and I were absolutely blown away when we first heard about Lisa LeCroy&rsquo;s designs!&nbsp; I immediately jumped on her website and was jazzed with the prospect of getting to design my own boots. (Boots I was obviously going to share with Clara.) Seriously?&nbsp; Who ever gets to design their own boots? There were so many combinations, and it was hard to make up my mind.&nbsp; The best part&hellip; everything is interchangeable! I chose my favorite heel height and bootie from the Black Collection and went with the Lucinda &ndash; black suede upper. The bootie alone is perfect for my every day outfits.&nbsp; Choosing the upper was the fun part.&nbsp; I knew that each one would give my bootie a completely different look.&nbsp; I finally settled on the Austin-suede whipstitch top because I loved the bows on the sides.&nbsp; I honestly spent over an hour just looking at all the combinations.&nbsp; Lisa LeCroy&rsquo;s website highlights them <a href="http://lisalecroy.com/collections/black-collection" target="_blank">all on one page</a> for easy viewing.</p>
<p>It was hard to capture the true beauty of these boots in a photo.&nbsp; They are super soft and luxurious.&nbsp; It&rsquo;s almost as if they melt to your feet and calves. The great thing about these boots is that I can take them with me when I travel.&nbsp; I can wear the bootie alone, plus with mix and match uppers, I&rsquo;ve got a different look each day without having to pack a million shoes.</p>
<p>Lisa LeCroy definitely had an &ldquo;Ah, Ha!&rdquo; moment when she came up with these boot designs.&nbsp; It&rsquo;s EVERY girl&rsquo;s dream to be able to switch up her boot look without having to buy a million separate pairs of books.&nbsp; Check out her fun video below, and you&rsquo;ll be hooked just like Clara and I now are!</p>
