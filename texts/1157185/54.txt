<p>Dressing &ldquo;age appropriate&rdquo; is becoming more and more like a thing of the past. Nowadays, it&rsquo;s all about how your carry yourself and your lifestyle. After all, age is just a number right? The point is to look classy and chic and have fun doing it.</p>
<p>A mother in her 40s may want a bit edgier look than a mother in her 20s. It&rsquo;s all about looking good and feeling confident! Here are my style tips for mothers of various different ages to look fabulous on Mother&rsquo;s Day.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/mothers.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72424];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72452" alt="Mother's Day" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/mothers.png" width="580" height="435"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<strong>20s</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Wear something fun and flirty! Crop tops are definitely in for Spring 2014!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>30s</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Sleek and sexy! Pink is certainly on trend for this upcoming Mother&rsquo;s Day!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<strong>40s</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Try something flowy and beautiful! Any kind of floral print will be excellent for this coming Spring!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<strong>50s</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Keep it young and vibrant! A beautiful color-blocked dress will be perfect for this Mother&rsquo;s Day!</p>
<p><strong>Things to avoid at any age:</strong></p>
<ul><li>Outfits that are too revealing</li>
<li>Outfits that don&rsquo;t fit you appropriately</li>
<li>Outfits that don&rsquo;t flatter your natural shape</li>
</ul><p>Remember! Shoes, handbags, and accessories are ageless pieces and can really make the outfit.</p>
