<p>As we make our transition from winter to spring, we have a few products that will be perfect for you and your family!</p>
<p>Dim the lights, throw a log on the fire, turn on some nice background music, and have the whole family enjoy some good old fashioned downtime &ndash; together &ndash; without electronics. Here are some items to help make this family time comfy and fun, as the weather starts getting warmer!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/CicciaBella.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72323];player=img;"><img class=" wp-image-72324 aligncenter" alt="From Winter to Spring" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/CicciaBella-1024x512.jpg" width="553" height="276"></a></p>
<p>Cozy feet are a must for an evening at home.<strong> CicciaBella</strong>&nbsp;slippers take your slipper choices to a whole new level of awesome.&nbsp;Designed for women and girls (with a few Rider styles available for boys as well), CicciaBella styles are sure to knock your socks off for the lasting days of winter. The women&rsquo;s Kwilt style, featuring a knit/chevron Strap and faux fur lining, described as the &ldquo;cuddliest breathable lining to keep your feet light as air,&rdquo; is simply adorable, and the Rider boots (that look like real cowboy boots) are so fun for women, girls and boys.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/CicciaBella2.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72323];player=img;"><img class=" wp-image-72327 aligncenter" alt="From Winter to Spring" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/CicciaBella2-1024x471.jpg" width="553" height="255"></a></p>
<p>I loved my colorful Riders! They definitely allowed my feet to survive during this season&rsquo;s cold winter in New York, especially with our number of snow days! What I also loved about my Riders was that I could easily slip them on and feel just perfect without having to wear socks. They were so snug, and I loved that they reached above my ankles. <a href="http://www.cicciabella.com/" target="_blank">www.cicciabella.com</a></p>

<p>What&rsquo;s more fun than a night in with family and friends playing board games?&nbsp;<strong>Endless Games</strong><b>&nbsp;</b>is always on the cutting edge of games that are fun for the whole family. Endless Games offers card, dice, party, word, strategy, trivia, survey and pop culture games for everyone! <b>Oddly Obvious</b> is the wild new party game where all the answers are right in front of you: One player reads clues on the back of a game card, while the other players race to spot the matching answers on the front.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/DSC_0920.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-72323];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter" alt="From Winter to Spring" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/DSC_0920-1024x687.jpg" width="553" height="371"></a></p>
<p>This game is great for all ages. It can even be used to build vocabulary in the classroom (as my aunt, a high school teacher, suggested to herself)! This game reminds me of my childhood, when I engaged with my other classmates on math problems that my teachers would hold up as game cards. I love(d) the thrill of winning! Not only that, this game can be taken outside of a house or classroom and played with while on the road. Because each game card acts as its own game (in contrast to full game sets only appropriate for home), Oddly Odvious is perfect for travel &mdash; simply hold up one game card at a time. <a href="http://www.endlessgames.com/" target="_blank">www.endlessgames.com</a></p>

<p>Lastly, as the weather gets warmer for spring, you&rsquo;ll be getting thirsty &amp; hungry for some frozen treats! These <strong>Arctic Zero</strong> treats are special &mdash; there are only 150 calories per pint! Crazy, right? You can gulf down a whole container without feeling guilty! Each container is gluten free, fat free, suitable for lactose intolerants, contains natural and organic ingredients, low glycemic &amp; suitable for Type 2 Diabetes, made with high quality whey protein, Kosher, non-GMO, 8 grams of fiber per pint, and great for kids (no sugar alcohols). A perfect treat for all family members! With so many flavors available, your taste buds will be satisfied. My favorite flavors were Vanilla Maple and Coffee!</p>
