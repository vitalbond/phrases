<p>For the past few years, Angelo David Salon has had the amazing idea of offering complimentary blowouts and manicures to fashion attendees during New York Fashion Week! Last February, as seen above, I was glammed up for the first time by the salon&rsquo;s staff.&nbsp;I loved visiting the salon and getting pampered from my head to my hands (red nails for the Month of Love!), and even to my stomach with such nice wine!</p>
<p><b>Angelo David Pisacreta (Celebrity Couture Hair Extension Expert)&nbsp;</b>is the owner and Creative Director of Angelo David Salon. He is a world-renowned stylist and colorist, as well as the originator of&nbsp;<b><i>Couture Hair Extensions &amp; Additions</i></b>, and the developer of The Angelo David Hair Care collection. By pairing his impeccable designing skills with artistic cutting, coloring and styling abilities, Angelo has transformed even the most challenging hair into lust-worthy locks.</p>
<p>Established in 2000, Angelo David Salon is located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan and just steps away from the beautiful Grand Central Terminal. Angelo David Salon offers a&nbsp;<b>full menu of personalized beauty treatments</b>&nbsp;for hair, skin, nails and lashes, as well as the&nbsp;<b>Angelo David Hair Care Line,</b><b>&nbsp;</b>an exclusive collection, specifically formulated to keep hair healthy, shiny and manageable. It includes&nbsp;<i>Vital Complex Leave-In Conditioner</i>,&nbsp;<i>Long &amp; Strong Detangler</i>,&nbsp;<i>Foaming Sea Silk Styler</i>,&nbsp;<i>VOL Shampoo and Conditioner, VOL Premier Volume Spray,</i><i>&nbsp;</i>and&nbsp;<i>Zero Frizz Control</i>, as well as&nbsp;<i>Hand-Crafted Natural Boar&rsquo;s Bristle Hair Brushes</i>, which keep natural and Couture Hair tangle-free. Below are some of the products that were used for my hair (click on images to view in large)!</p>
