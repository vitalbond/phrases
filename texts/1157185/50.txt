<p>Personally, I like the idea of a self discovery journal&hellip; Oh, and finding a <a title="Giveaway:Stylish Notepads by Bonnie Marcus" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2010/05/giveawaystylish-notepads-by-bonnie-marcus/">great notebook</a> that inspires you and makes you excited to capture your thoughts down on paper.</p>
<p><strong>So let me ask you this&hellip; What are your gifts or talents?</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72640" alt="Your Gifts" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/ws.jpg" width="580" height="836"><br><em>I will start&hellip;</em></p>
<p><em>My first thought was of my daughter (she is my gift).</em></p>
<p>So, then I decided to ask my daughter, now a beautiful, amazing young woman&hellip; I asked her what she thought my gifts were. This was her reply:</p>
<p>&ldquo;You have an innate ability to meet, relate to, and inspire people to achieve a higher quality of life and to help others realize their own potential.&rdquo;</p>
<p>Wow!! That was pretty awesome&hellip; and this is true&hellip; hence the reason I became interested in wellness and being a life coach. I realized how important it was to be an enthusiast in life.&nbsp;I love the opportunity to meet amazing and dynamic women. I am passionate about sparking the best in individuals (holding the mirror up), so we are able to see our own beauty.</p>
<p>Ask yourself, &ldquo;What&rsquo;s the biggest passion in my life?&rdquo; Then? Go do that. Because that&rsquo;s the moment when your life turns from being a question mark into being an exclamation point.</p>
