<div><p style="text-align: center;">I believe… every day is a new day to start again. To manifest anything you desire.</p>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;">I love this saying…”Don’t let a bad day <strong>make you feel you</strong> have a bad life.”</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>“NOTHING IS IMPOSSIBLE. THE WORD ITSELF SPELLS ‘I’M POSSIBLE’!”</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/068b05ec5bd3d26ca24f827749b15656.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-73644];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-73645" alt="tomorrow" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/068b05ec5bd3d26ca24f827749b15656.jpg" width="580" height="990"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>DO THIS FOR YOURSELF</strong></p>
<p><b>Start your day off with a positive attitude…</b> I don’t care if you have to sit there for 15 minutes until you finally say five good things about yourself. If you wake up in a bad mood, do it! You will soon see when you start your day on a positive note, and you will be happy.</p>
<p><b>Respect yourself… </b>we all have a moral compass that tells us what we should and shouldn’t do. Respect yourself enough to listen to it.</p>
<p><b>Exercise… </b>this forces the body to produce endorphins, which helps you feel happy.</p>
<p><b>Indulge in your passion… </b>find out what makes you happy, and do those things!</p>
<p><b></b><b>Take me time… </b>set aside at least 15 minutes just for yourself. Spend it doing something that relaxes you and makes you feel good.</p>
<p><b>Dress up… </b>on your worst days, when you want to stay in bed all day and eat Oreos, ice cream, and watch movies… DON’T. Shower, put on cute clothes, makeup and a great hairstyle. Oh…and don’t forget your ‘lips’. Something about looking good makes you feel good.</p>
<p> </p>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;">“Yesterday is gone, Tomorrow has not come, We only have Today. Let us begin.”<br>
- Mother Teresa</p>
</blockquote>
