<div><p>Spice up your Cinco de Mayo party this year with celebratory concoctions from the makers of the &ldquo;World&rsquo;s Best Tasting Tequila,&rdquo; <a title="Toast St. Patrick&rsquo;s Day with Tequila Avi&oacute;n" href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/2014/03/toast-st-patricks-day-with-tequila-avion/">Tequila Avi&oacute;n</a>.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72576" alt="Cinco de Mayo" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/image007.jpg" width="311" height="545"><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Avi&oacute;n (PO) Loma</span></b></p>
<p>1.5 oz. Avi&oacute;n Silver</p>
<p>&frac12; oz. Lime Juice</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Lemon Juice</p>
<p>3 oz. Grapefruit Juice</p>
<p>2 Slivers Muddled Jalape&ntilde;o</p>
<p>Soda Water</p>
<p>Ancho Chile Salt, Jalape&ntilde;o, and Grilled Lemon Wedge for garnish</p>
<p><i>Combine all ingredients and shake. Salt rim of glass with chile salt. Pour contents of shaker into glass, and top with soda. Garnish with grilled lemon and jalape&ntilde;o. </i></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72577" alt="Cinco de Mayo" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/image009.jpg" width="367" height="497"><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">The Mariachi</span></b></p>
<p>1 &frac12; oz. Avi&oacute;n Reposado</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Black Peach Tea</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. White Peach Puree</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Lemon Juice</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Brown Sugar</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Cranberry Juice</p>
<p>3 Blackberries</p>
<p>1 Blackberry and Lemon Wheel for garnish</p>
<p><i>Muddle blackberries and brown sugar in shaker. Add all ingredients and shake. Double strain, and pour into a glass over ice. Garnish with 1 blackberry and a lemon wheel.</i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72578" alt="Cinco de Mayo" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/image011.jpg" width="368" height="506"></p>
<p><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Party Thyme Punch</span></b></p>
<p>1 &frac12; oz. Avi&oacute;n Reposado</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Lime Juice</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Lemon Juice</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Grenadine</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Simple Syrup</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Pomegranate Juice</p>
<p>1 tsp. All Spice</p>
<p>1 sprig Thyme for garnish</p>
<p><i>Combine all ingredients and ice into a shaker, excluding the all spice. Shake and strain into a glass over ice. Sprinkle all spice on top. Garnish with sprig of thyme. </i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72579" alt="Cinco de Mayo" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/image013.jpg" width="397" height="597"></p>
<p><b><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Rita Rioja</span></b></p>
<p>&frac34; oz.&nbsp;Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice</p>
<div>
<p>&frac34; oz.&nbsp;Simple Syrup (equal parts sugar and water)</p>
</div>
<p>1&frac12; oz.&nbsp;Tequila Avi&oacute;n Reposado</p>
<p>Fresh Red Pepper</p>
<p>Cilantro</p>
<p><i>Rub the rim of a glass with a wedge of fresh lime and dip into some salt. Add some spice to the margarita by muddling a piece of red pepper and a little bit of cilantro to bring out their natural flavors. Add 1&frac12; ounces Tequila Avi&oacute;n Reposado and &frac34; ounce each of fresh lime juice and simple syrup. Then fill mixing glass with ice. Give the cocktail a good shake to chill, and strain it into a salted glass. Garnish with a piece of red pepper and cilantro. </i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-72580" alt="Cinco de Mayo" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Avion-Bloody-Maria-853x1024.jpg" width="391" height="468"></p>
<p><b>Bloody Maria</b><b> </b></p>
<p>1 &frac12; parts Avi&oacute;n Silver</p>
<p>4 parts Tomato Juice</p>
<p>2 tsp Horseradish</p>
<p>&frac12; part Worcestershire Sauce</p>
<p>2 Dashes Hot Sauce</p>
<p>Squeeze Lemon</p>
<p>Squeeze Lime</p>
<p>Salt and Pepper</p>
<p><i>Combine ingredients in a Collins glass with ice, and stir. Do not shake. Garnish with olives and a celery stick.</i></p>
