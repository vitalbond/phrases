<div><p style="text-align: left;" align="center">Taking care of our skin to keep it looking healthy &amp; beautiful is a constant reminder that we must make to ourselves!</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong>First Aid Beauty Facial Radiance Pads</strong> can help you keep your skin clean, tone and bright.</p>

<p style="text-align: left;" align="center">Exfoliating every day helps new skin cells surface. Facial pads make this an easy task in the morning and before bed. The pads are convenient to use. No more scrubbing a rough washcloth on your face to exfoliate. That can be hard on the skin. All you need to do is gently wipe a pad across your face and neck once or twice a day to see results.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center">I noticed a difference after a few days of using the facial pads. They really make my skin feel clean before bed and in the morning. My skin feels smooth and more tone.</p>
