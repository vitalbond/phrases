<p>Love new trends? Love jewelry? And love animals? Then Wrist Critters are for you! Get on board with the up and coming trend of these fashionable bracelets. The initial collection of Wrist Critters contains 6 animal characters: Asian + African Elephants, Black Cat, Chameleon, Goldfish, Skulls, and White Mouse. Each animal has been chosen for its individual story, which comes on back of the collecting case.</p>
<p><b>Wrist Critters&trade;&nbsp;</b>are designed by Mark Dornan, who, along with his wife Rita, have been operating a sculpture-based design / manufacturing business since 1979 located at the New Jersey Shore.</p>
<p>In that time, they have produced pieces which span a number of interests: Fashion and Precious Jewelry / 3-D Art &amp; Collectables / Limited edition bronze sculpture / Custom furniture, and Musical instruments. Mark&rsquo;s work includes designing and creating sculptural pieces in collaboration with Disney, Warner Bros., Marc Jacobs, The Hard Rock Caf&eacute;, The &nbsp;Fashion Caf&eacute; and ESPN Zone.</p>
<p>Colorful and fashionable, these bracelets are the perfect outfit accessory for spring. Dress an outfit up with these bracelets or make a statement &mdash; they can be styled many different ways.</p>
