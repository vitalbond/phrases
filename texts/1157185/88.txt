<div><p style="text-align: center;">Welcome to my Healthy Travel Guide Series!</p>
<p>While traveling, our routines are hard-pressed, and while we may gain a lot mentally or spiritually on our journey, we can also lose sight of our healthy selves. Although your schedule may be quite busy while on a trip, take five to thirty minutes to break a sweat, and keep your body at its best.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/ALPmove_quote.jpg" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-73153];player=img;"><img class="wp-image-73158 aligncenter" alt="healthy travel guide" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/ALPmove_quote.jpg" width="579" height="385"></a></p>
<p><em>The Travel Workout by Move.Nourish.Believe </em></p>
<p>5 Minutes, 1000 Reps (<em>What’s your excuse not to?</em>)</p>
<p>+ Mountain Climbers<br>
+ Ab Cycles<br>
+ Tuck Jumps<br>
+ Ice Skaters</p>
<p>Put them into sets (<em>for a challenge do three times the sets</em>):</p>
<p><strong>Set 1</strong></p>
<p>+ Mountain Climbers x 10<br>
+ Ab Cycles x 10</p>
<p><strong>60 second break</strong></p>
<p><strong>Set 2</strong></p>
<p>+ Tuck Jumps x 5<br>
+ Ice Skaters x 10</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://ohyesletithappen.tumblr.com/post/76831767741"><img class="wp-image-73156 aligncenter" alt="healthy travel guide" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/yoga.jpg" width="579" height="483"></a></p>

<p>Whether you bring a mat on your travels or use a hotel room towel, the wonderful thing about yoga is that it is completely customizable. You can target different areas, ranging in different amounts of time, and for multiple intentions (i.e. stress, sleep, strength, etc.).</p>
