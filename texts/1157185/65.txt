<div><p>NBA Playoffs only come once a year, so take this opportunity to be sporty and fashionable this year. It&rsquo;s tough looking cute at sporting events, but we have you covered!&nbsp;Neff and Unk have teamed up with the NBA to create a&nbsp;limited run of tee shirts for the 2014 Playoffs.&nbsp;The product line is a&nbsp;mix of Neff&rsquo;s wild style with the&nbsp;classic logos of the NBA&rsquo;s top teams. From the Chicago Bulls to the Los Angeles Clippers, Neff has designed a new take on your favorite team.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Here in Oklahoma, there is only one team I cheer for!!!</p>

<p><b><i>&ldquo;As a huge NBA fan, I couldn&rsquo;t be more excited about designing tee shirts for my favorite NBA teams,<b><i>&rdquo;</i></b> says Neff Founder and CEO, Shaun Neff. &ldquo;Bringing our unique flavor and design to the world&rsquo;s biggest basketball teams is an honor, and I can&rsquo;t wait to wear them during the playoffs.&rdquo;</i></b></p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">Neff is all about having fun and bringing new vibes to the world. &ldquo;We are like a gumball machine; spitting out endless flavors for the world to consume,&rdquo; stated Neff, when asked to describe Neff Headwear. &ldquo;We are not confined to one specific category; we are defined by reaching across multiple platforms like sports, fashion and entertainment.&rdquo;</span></p>
<p>The collection will be featured through various Neff retailers and sold on&nbsp;<a href="http://www.neffheadwear.com/shop/NBA" target="_blank">www.neffheadwear.com/shop/NBA</a>.&nbsp; The tees will retail for a price of $30.00 and will be available during the 2014 NBA playoffs.</p>
