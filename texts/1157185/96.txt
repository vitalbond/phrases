<p><strong>John Russo Beauty </strong>is a leader in a world of followers. In our celebrity driven culture, John developed his own line of luxury products and leveraged his celebrity relationships to successfully develop and grow his brand. His bold and dynamic products allow consumers to experience the world of celebrity. John named his <a href="http://www.johnrussobeauty.com/nail-laquer/" target="_blank">nail polish colors</a> after the people, places and events that have shaped his life.</p>
<p>Only until now, John had privately distributed his coveted beauty products to celebrity clients. Worn by Hollywood’s a-listers, Gwyneth Paltrow, Abbie Cornish, Nicole Richie, Cindy Crawford, Zoe Saldana, Christina Aguilera and Evan Rachel Wood. Now the public is able to purchase his bold nail lacquer in person at the <a href="http://www.montagehotels.com/beverlyhills/" target="_blank">Montage Beverly Hills</a>, the Montage Maui, the Four Seasons Santa Barbara and other various luxury boutiques.</p>

<p id="yui_3_10_1_1_1401641114436_240">John Russo is one of the top celebrity &#38; fashion photographers in the world. His work has graced the covers of Vogue, Elle, Harper’s Bazaar, Glamour, Cosmopolitan, GQ &#38; Esquire. He has photographed campaigns for Hugo Boss, Dolce &#38; Gabbana, Gucci and Lacoste. John has shot Angelina Jolie, Gwyneth Paltrow, Brad Pitt, Leonardo DiCaprio &#38; Julianne Moore to name a few.</p>
