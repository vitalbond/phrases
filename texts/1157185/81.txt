<div><p>I&nbsp;chatted with 18-year-old singer/songwriter, performing and recording artist, and <a class="zem_slink" title="Canadian Museums Association" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=45.4158333333,-75.6913888889&amp;spn=0.01,0.01&amp;q=45.4158333333,-75.6913888889%20(Canadian%20Museums%20Association)&amp;t=h" target="_blank" rel="geolocation">CMA</a> Member, Dani-elle for an in-depth look on her career aspirations, charity work and of course, fashion!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/p.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-73491];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73492" alt="Dani-elle" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/p.png" width="387" height="580"></a></p>
<p><em>Tell us about your early beginnings and how you got into singing!</em></p>
<p>I began singing at the age of 3 in church, with the song, &ldquo;Angels Among Us.&rdquo; I was unable to read yet, so I told them if they would give my Mommy the words, then I would learn them. And that I did. That was the first time I performed out in front of an audience and from that day forward, I loved it. I knew that was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. Throughout the years, I have won many talent competitions and decided that this was something that I really wanted to do with my life. I began performing regularly at the age of 9 and took my first trip to <a class="zem_slink" title="Nashville, Tennessee" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=36.1666666667,-86.7833333333&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=36.1666666667,-86.7833333333%20(Nashville%2C%20Tennessee)&amp;t=h" target="_blank" rel="geolocation">Nashville</a> with my parents at the age of 12. My parents are my number one fans, support system, and best friends. They have been beside me since day one, when I said I wanted to be a singer, and always support my dream. I am forever grateful for them. I am where I am today because of my amazing parents.</p>
<p><em>You&rsquo;re very active with charities and giving back. Tell us a bit more about&nbsp;what it means to you!</em></p>
<p>I believe in helping others, and when I see a charity that I believe in, I am more than willing to help. God blessed me with this gift, and it truly is amazing that I get to use it to make a difference in the world. I have seen music change people&rsquo;s lives. So therefore, charity is very important to me.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/IMG_7870.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-73491];player=img;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-73493" alt="Dani-elle" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/IMG_7870.png" width="387" height="580"></a></p>
<p><em>How is the future looking in terms of your career goals?</em></p>
<p>My aspirations are to keep doing what I am already doing. I am only 18 now and am fortunate enough to say that I am living my dream already. I am working towards a tour and eventually becoming part of the Main Stream Country Music Line Up. I am blessed with wonderful fans, who are dedicated in coming out to shows, loving my music, and believing that a small town girl can fulfill her big dreams in the country music world!! I am further more blessed with my Dani-elle Dream Team support system.</p>
<p><em>I hear you have a jewelry line?&nbsp;</em></p>
<p>I have always been big into fashion. Everyone would always ask after seeing me on stage, &ldquo;Where did you get your jewelry?&rdquo;&nbsp;So we started the&nbsp;Dani-elle&nbsp;designer jewelry line, in which we have things such as bracelets, necklaces, scarves, and so much more!! Recently, we became in contact with Studio Penny Lane, after my Mom had bought me one of their bracelets. We continued to get in contact with them after realizing that they too, had a penny from heaven story just like myself. We now carry their penny jewelry in my line, as I am always sporting their beautiful penny jewelry on and off stage. We feel so blessed and fortunate to be working with Laurie and Ashley and the whole team from Studio Penny Lane, as we team up to share our penny stories and inspire gratitude one penny at a time!!</p>
