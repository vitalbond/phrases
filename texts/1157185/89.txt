<p>Are you wearing heels this summer? According to the <a href="http://www.osteopathic.org/osteopathic-health/about-your-health/health-conditions-library/womens-health/Pages/high-heels.aspx" target="_blank">American Osteopathic Association</a>, “One in ten women wears high heels at least three days a week.” In addition, high heels are one of the “biggest factors leading to foot problems in women, with up to a third suffering permanent problems as a result of prolonged wear.” As a solution, <a href="http://www.aerosoles.com/" target="_blank"><b>AEROSOLES</b></a>, <b>a leading shoe brand offering over 20 years of experience in the footwear industry, </b>has developed heels with “Heel Rest” technology and “Strobel” advancements. The Heel Rest distributes weight towards the heel and away from the ball of the foot, resulting in superior comfort. Strobel is a design technique in which the insole board at the toe is stitched instead of glued, adding extra flexibility.</p>
