Toast St. Patrick&rsquo;s Day this year with premium cocktails from Tequila Avi&oacute;n.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><b>The Irish Espresso </b></p>
<p>Bartenders call it: The Red Eye</p>
<p>1 part Avi&oacute;n Espresso</p>
<p>1 part Jameson Whiskey</p>
<p><i>Pour ingredients into a rocks glass over ice. Stir.</i></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-72200" alt="Tequila Avi&oacute;n" src="http://www.chicgalleria.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/image005.jpg" width="364" height="455"><b>Spicy St. Patrick Sage Margarita</b></p>
<p>2 oz. Avi&oacute;n Silver</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Jalape&ntilde;o-Infused Elderflower Liqueur</p>
<p>&frac12; oz. Orange Juice</p>
<p>&frac14; oz. Lemon Juice</p>
<p>&frac12; oz. Lime Juice</p>
<p>&frac34; oz. Simple Syrup</p>
<p>3 Sage Leaves</p>
<p>1 Sage Leaf and Salt for garnish</p>
<p><i>Muddle sage in a shaker. Add all ingredients. Shake. Double strain. Pour over ice in salt-rimmed glass. Garnish with sage leaf.</i></p>
