What I&rsquo;m going to teach you&hellip;</p>
<p><strong>Basics of nutrition</strong><br>
- What is protein<br>
- What is fat<br>
- What are carbs</p>
<p><strong>Good proteins</strong><br>
- Grass fed, free range&hellip;<br>
Why:<br>
- We are what we eat eats!</p>
<p><strong>Good fats</strong><br>
- Grass fed, free range&hellip;<br>
Why:<br>
- Quality fats come from animals that eat greens. Think fish.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Good carbs</strong><br>
Good question.&nbsp;<br>
- Carbs are good only at the right time.&nbsp;<br>
- Carbs are good only at the right what?<br>
- Would you put gas into a car with a full tank?</p>
<p><strong>How often to eat&hellip;</strong><br>
- When are you hungry?<br>
- A good rule of thumb is every 3 hours.&nbsp;<br>
When is a bad time to eat?<br>
- Never!<br>
- All that matters is what and timing.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Micro meal timing (one meal)</strong><br>
- How to think&hellip; Proteins and fats first.&nbsp;<br>
- Then carbs (if necessary).&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Macro meal timing (full day)</strong><br>
- When do you workout?<br>
- Does your body know that it needs carbs before you workout?</p>
<p><strong>&ndash;Post workout nutrition!&ndash;</strong><br>
- quick digesting protein and sugar.<br>
- I use Progenex and something with dextrose (Gatorade, or Now Foods Dextrose powder). </p>
<p><strong>What are your goals?</strong><br>
- Weight/fat loss?<br>
- Performance?<br>
- Both?</p>
<p><strong>Where to shop&hellip;</strong><br>
- At the farm or butcher.&nbsp;<br>
- Source your foods.&nbsp;<br>
- Buy a cow with friends.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Water</strong><br>
- .75 ounces per pound of body weight.&nbsp;</p>
