<h2 id="release">Release News</h2>
<h3>Pure</h3>
<p>The Pure team released <a href="https://github.com/yui/pure/releases/tag/v0.5.0-rc-1">Pure 0.5.0 Release Candidate 1</a> this week. This RC includes a complete overhaul of Pure&rsquo;s current Grid system, and replaces it with a new mobile-first responsive grid.</p>
<p>There have also been new additions to the <a href="http://stage.purecss.io/">Pure Website</a>, including a brand new <a href="http://stage.purecss.io/start/">Getting Started</a> page and new <a href="http://stage.purecss.io/grids/">Grids Documentation</a>. Since this is an RC release, you can find the new changes over on the <a href="http://stage.purecss.io/">staging website</a>.</p>
<p>Let us know what you think of the new changes! We&rsquo;ll squash any remaining bugs and release Pure 0.5.0 shortly.</p>
