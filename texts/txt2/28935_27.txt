CDN</a> for you to include directly in your scripts, taking advantage of Yahoo!&rsquo;s fast CDN. You can also <a href="https://npmjs.org/package/yui">install it via npm</a> or <a href="http://yui.zenfs.com/releases/yui3/yui_3.12.0.zip">download it directly</a>. We&rsquo;ve also updated the <a href="http://yuilibrary.com/">YUI Library website</a> to reflect the changes in this release.</p>
