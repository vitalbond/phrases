<h2 id="release-news">Release News</h2>
<h3>YUI</h3>
<p>We&rsquo;re getting closer to releasing the next version of YUI, which will either be 3.16.0, or 3.15.1, depending on what code has landed in the library. The <a href="https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Development-Schedule">code freeze</a> deadline is scheduled for March 28th. If you&rsquo;re contributing any code for this release, you&rsquo;ll want to get your Pull Requests in by March 25th!</p>
<p>Our next release is slated to go out on April 8th.</p>
<h2 id="open-roundtable">YUI Open Roundtable</h2>
<p>In his week&rsquo;s <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WiNt_XMGrwA">YUI Open Roundtable</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/clarler">Clarence</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/jetfault">Jerry</a> and <a href="http://www.yuiblog.com/twitter.com/triptych">Andrew</a> sat down with Lauren Tsung from Yahoo. Lauren has a CS background but is currently a Design Prototyper at Yahoo who focuses on User Experience. Their conversation focussed on ways that designers and developers can collaborate together to make better products.</p>
