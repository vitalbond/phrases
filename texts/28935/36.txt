<ul><li>
<p>This week marks the conclusion of <a href="https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Development-Schedule#sprint-10-next-release">Sprint 10</a>&lsquo;s primary development cycle. At the beginning of next week, a release branch will be cut from dev-3.x and <strong>YUI 3.13.0-rc-1</strong> will be released shortly thereafter. After a week of testing and showstopper bug fixing, the release for <strong>YUI 3.13.0</strong> will be targeted for Tuesday, October 8th.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>There&rsquo;s only 7 weeks until <a href="http://yuilibrary.com/yuiconf/">YUIConf 2013</a>! This week the Call for Speakers deadline was announced, so if you have a talk you&rsquo;d like to propose, email your idea to <em>yui-events (at) yahoo-inc.com</em> by <strong>October 11th</strong>. Check out <a href="http://www.yuiblog.com/blog/2013/09/26/yuiconf-2013-call-for-speakers-deadline/">the announcement</a> for more details.</p>
</li>
