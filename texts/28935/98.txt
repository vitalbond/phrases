<h2 id="release">Release News</h2>
<h3>YUI</h3>
<p>We&rsquo;ll be shipping YUI 3.17.0 next week! <a href="https://github.com/yui/yui3/wiki/Development-Schedule">Sprint 14</a> has ended and we&rsquo;ll be doing a last round of checks on Monday before deploying the release branch. If all goes well, YUI 3.17.0 will be out on Tuesday morning!</p>
<h3>Pure</h3>
<p>We&rsquo;re planning on shipping Pure 0.5.0 this week as well. We&rsquo;ve had the <a href="http://stage.purecss.io/">Pure 0.5.0 Release Candidate 1</a> out for a while now and haven&rsquo;t noticed any regressions so we&rsquo;ll be going ahead with the 0.5.0 GA release. Stay tuned by following us on the <a href="http://blog.purecss.io/">Pure Blog</a>.</p>
