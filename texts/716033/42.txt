They were very impressed with our quick response in getting doors locked and lights off. The entire school was lockdown within seconds.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Holy Cross Catholic Church is in the midst of a Capital Campaign. All parishioners are asked to make a pledge to the campaign. Please prayerfully consider giving. If you have questions regarding the campaign, please contact me, or Bob Bacic in the parish office. Remember, the parish budget contributes forty percent toward your child&rsquo;s education.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Our virtue for the month of March is humility. As I researched prayers for humility, I came across this list written by Mother Teresa. The list opened my eyes and touched my heart. I pray they are insightful for you as well.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><span style="color:#333333;"><strong>Mother Teresa&rsquo;s Humility List</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">1. Speak as little as possible about yourself.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">2. Keep busy with your own affairs and not those of others.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">3. Avoid curiosity.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">4. Do not interfere in the affairs of others.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">5. Accept small irritations with good humor.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">6. Do not dwell on the faults of others.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">7. Accept censures even if unmerited.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">8. Give in to the will of others.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">9. Accept insults and injuries.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">10. Accept contempt, being forgotten and disregarded.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">11. Be courteous and delicate even when provoked by someone.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">12. Do not seek to be admired and loved.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">13. Do not protect yourself behind your own dignity.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">14. Give in, in discussions, even when you are right.</span><br><span style="color:#333333;">15. Choose always the more difficult task.</span></p>
