<p><span style="color:#000000;">I have given many tours of our wonderful school over the past few months. During those tours, I am often asked about our priorities at Holy Cross. My first response is that our highest responsibility is to help our children reach Heaven. I pray every day for our students, families, and staff, that God guides us all to his loving arms.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">The next three days, the Easter Triduum, is the summit of the Liturgical Year. The three days, considered a single celebration, marks the end of Lent. On Saturday at the Easter Vigil, two of our second grade parents, and one of our second grade students will be welcomed into the Church. Amen! I hope to see you and your family at the Triduum.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">We are so thankful for the wonderful parents of Holy Cross Catholic School. We are blessed to partner with you in educating your children. Please stop downstairs after our All School Mass next Friday for coffee and donuts. Please accept this small gesture given with great love for your unending support.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">God bless,</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Allison</span></p>
