<div><p><span style="color:#000000;">Dear Parents,<a href="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2014/04/hcnewsletter-140411.pdf"><span style="color:#000000;"><img class="alignright wp-image-1680 size-thumbnail" src="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2010/09/nutshell.jpg?w=136&amp;h=150" alt="Holy Cross newsletter in a nutshell" width="136" height="150"></span></a></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">I must admit I am not adept at social media. A few months ago, my son set up a Twitter account for me. Much to my surprise, I learned Pope Francis was tweeting, so I began to follow him. If you are interested, you can follow him on Twitter &ndash; <strong><a href="https://twitter.com/pontifex" target="_blank"><span style="color:#000000;">@pontifex</span></a></strong>. His daily tweets are inspirational. </span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">On April 1, the Pope tweeted, &ldquo;Dear parents, teach your children to pray. Pray with them.&rdquo; As the end of Lent nears, I pray you heed the message from Pope Francis and pray with your children. Your example of prayer will set the tone for a faith filled and loving home for your family. Below is a prayer for families you may wish to use to begin a daily time of prayer with your children.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;"><span style="color:#000000;"><strong><em>O dear Jesus, we humbly implore You to grant Your special graces to our family. May our home be the shrine of peace, purity, love and faith. We beg You, dear Jesus, to protect and bless all of us, absent and present, living and deceased. We ask you this through Christ our Lord. Amen.</em></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">One benefit of daily prayer is the strength it provides as I approach the many activities pulling at my time. This is definitely a busy time of year for all of us. See the following page for a list of upcoming events at Holy Cross. I hope to see you enjoy the wonderful community spirit that is alive and well at Holy Cross.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">God bless,</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Allison</span></p>
