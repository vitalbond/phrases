<p><span style="color:#000000;">In this Octave of Easter, I had the privilege of attending the National Catholic Educator Conference in Pittsburgh, PA. Many of you know that Pittsburgh is my hometown. It was a blessing to experience all the conference had to offer, and visit my brothers, sisters, nieces and nephews in the same visit. God is good!</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Attending daily mass celebrated by Cardinal Wuerl and Bishop Zubick of Pittsburgh was definitely the highlight of the conference. Both stated that the Catholic school was the place for each student to encounter the living God, and they challenged us to weave a relationship with Christ into the fabric of every child. Our message should be one that inspires young people to fall in love with Jesus and His church. I am happy to say that Holy Cross Catholic School is living that challenge.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Congratulations to our talented students for another spectacular Spring Concert. Although I wasn&rsquo;t in attendance, I received many pictures and videos of the evening. A special thank you to Melissa Cain, Christy Kincaid, and Kim Siurek for leading our students.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">God bless,</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Allison</span></p>
