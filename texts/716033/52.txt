<p><strong>~~~~~~~~~~~~~<br><span style="color:#333333;"><em>That said, you all know what Scrip is, right?&nbsp; If not, read on&hellip;</em></span></strong></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;"><strong></strong>It&rsquo;s an easy way to help raise money for Holy Cross,<br>
and it&rsquo;s a win-win proposition.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;"><a href="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/scrip.jpg"><span style="color:#333333;"><img class="alignright" style="border:0 none;margin:10px;" title="scrip" alt="" src="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/scrip.jpg?w=150&amp;h=101" width="150" height="101"></span></a>SCRIP is basically gift cards that you buy from the parish/school. You buy them at face value, and a percentage of the purchase is donated to Holy Cross (we can buy them at a reduced price &ndash; the percentage varies from store to store.)</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">LOTS of businesses where you currently shop are on the list, I&rsquo;m sure. There are restaurants, gas station, grocery stores, retail of all sorts, online shopping (including SchoolMall, but that&rsquo;s a whole other blog post!) The SCRIP list is long. Pick up a form at the church or school, if you&rsquo;re interested &ndash; or download the forms online (form for <a href="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2014/03/scrip_instock.pdf" target="_blank"><span style="color:#333333;">SCRIP we keep in stock all the time</span></a> and form for <a href="http://holycrosscatholicschool.files.wordpress.com/2014/03/scrip_byorder.pdf" target="_blank"><span style="color:#333333;">SCRIP we sometimes have to order</span></a>).</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">You can choose to have the entire percentage donated to Holy Cross, or you can split the percentage. If you have a child at Holy Cross School, you can put your percentage of the SCRIP purchase toward your child&rsquo;s tuition the following year (We tabulate your SCRIP purchases all year, and you get a statement in May of your total. At that time, you can choose to donate it to the Guardian Angel Fund or your child&rsquo;s tuition.)</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">To give you an idea of the financial reward of the Scrip program for Holy Cross, think about this:</span></p>
<ul><li><span style="color:#333333;">If one family buys $100 of Hy-Vee SCRIP each week for a year, </span><span style="color:#333333;">the return is: $260 (5%)</span></li>
<li><span style="color:#333333;">If one family buys $50 of Penney&rsquo;s or Kohl&rsquo;s SCRIP each month for a year,</span><span style="color:#333333;"> the return is: $30 (5%) &ndash;</span><span style="color:#333333;"> AND you can even use Kohl&rsquo;s SCRIP to pay your Kohl&rsquo;s charge!</span></li>
<li><span style="color:#333333;">If one family buys $100 of CVS, Walgreen&rsquo;s, or Wal-Mart SCRIP </span><span style="color:#333333;">each month for a year, the return is: $24 (2%)</span></li>
<li><span style="color:#333333;">If one family does all of the above, their &ldquo;extra&rdquo; annual contribution </span><span style="color:#333333;">to Holy Cross is: $314</span></li>
<li><span style="color:#333333;">If 200 families did this, the extra annual contribution to Holy Cross </span><span style="color:#333333;">would be $62,800</span></li>
</ul><p><span style="color:#333333;">The SCRIP cards make great gifts.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">They&rsquo;re also a good tool for budgeting. (How much is your family&rsquo;s monthly grocery budget? Spend that for SCRIP to your grocery store and that&rsquo;s it. That&rsquo;s what you get for the month. Makes it easier to stick with it, some say.)</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Bottomline, you&rsquo;re going to buy groceries and gas, right? Get into the habit of thinking ahead and buying SCRIP to make your purchase. Like I said, win-win.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">You can purchase the SCRIP cards at Holy Cross School during the week and at Holy Cross Church before and after mass on the weekend. The organizers make it really easy. You can even send your SCRIP order form in via backpack mail along with your check, and your cards will be returned to you within days.</span></p><br><a rel="nofollow" href="http://feeds.wordpress.com/1.0/gocomments/holycrosscatholicschool.wordpress.com/10797/"><img alt="" border="0" src="http://feeds.wordpress.com/1.0/comments/holycrosscatholicschool.wordpress.com/10797/"></a> <img alt="" border="0" src="http://stats.wordpress.com/b.gif?host=holycrosscatholicschool.wordpress.com&amp;blog=4224641&amp;post=10797&amp;subd=holycrosscatholicschool&amp;ref=&amp;feed=1" width="1" height="1"></div>
