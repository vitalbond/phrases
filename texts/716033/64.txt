<p><span style="color:#333333;">Last night, the students from Ms. Marx&rsquo;s drama class presented The Pony Expresso by Rachel Davidson. The audience was thoroughly entertained by the comedic performance of the cast that included Ali Stallbaumer, Allie Canfield, Devin Palmer, Sean Ryan, Elayna Marquis, Ben Smith, Jarod Roberts, Jillian Adams, Katie Hillyer, and Mickie Simmons. Benn Vavak&rsquo;s sound effects added to the performance. Congratulations to Ms. Marx and the cast for a great show!</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Do you need a new book? Stop by Barnes and Noble at Oak Park Mall on Saturday from 9:00 a.m. &ndash; 5:00 p.m. to make your purchase. The Holy Cross 6th grade band is playing at 10:15 a.m., and the 5th grade band will play at 11:30 a.m. Artwork from Holy Cross students will also be displayed. Holy Cross library will receive proceeds from every purchase. Please present the voucher attached to this newsletter to identify your support for Holy Cross.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Today we met the future of Holy Cross. Thirty-five future kindergarten students arrived today eager to meet their new teachers and see their new classrooms. We are looking forward to a fantastic class of 2023. If you are interested in being an ambassador to a new family, please contact the office. We want to extend our warmest welcome to our new families.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">The safety of your children is of utmost importance. Please be attentive when entering and leaving the parking lots. Also, please use the car line to pick up your children. Children are not permitted to cross the car line without an adult. Children are not permitted to play in the parking lot until all cars have exited. Do not turn left when leaving the parking lot at dismissal. This is very dangerous and causes delays in our pick up procedures.</span></p>

<p><span style="color:#333333;">God bless,</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">Allison</span></p>
