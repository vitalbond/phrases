<p><span style="color:#333333;">Holy Cross fifth grade students along with nine hundred students from the Archdiocese of Kansas City in Kansas met at Prince of Peace for a Vocation Day.&nbsp; Students spent the day exploring religious life and priesthood vocations.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#333333;">The day began with praise and song then breaking out into mini sessions, which included praying the rosary and discussions with priests and sisters.&nbsp; The day concluded with Archbishop Naumann and the celebration of mass.</span></p>
