<div><p>I&rsquo;m afraid that I haven&rsquo;t been following the release of <em>Conception: II Children of the Seven Stars</em> too closely as of recent, but I do know that it is under development by Atlus Games, the same developers responsible for the <em>Shin Megami Tensai</em> series and <em>Catherine</em>.&nbsp; Today, Atlus has released the game&rsquo;s official demo on the Playstation Store and Nintendo&nbsp;eShop for free download.</p>
