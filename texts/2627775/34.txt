<div><p><a title="Telltale Games Forums" href="http://www.telltalegames.com/community/discussion/58355/episode-3-release-dates-48-pcmac-ps3-north-america-49-xbox-360-ps3-eu-ios-next-week-too" target="_blank">The Official Telltale Forums</a> have confirmed the release dates for the third episode of their highly successful episodic series <em>The Wolf Among Us</em>.&nbsp; The episode will get released to PC and North American PS3&nbsp;first on&nbsp;April 8th.&nbsp; All Xbox 360 and International PS3 users will be able to download the episode on April 9th.&nbsp; The iOS version&nbsp;doesn&rsquo;t have an exact release date just yet, but users should expect it next week as well.</p>
