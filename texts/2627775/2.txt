<p><a title="Bungie.net" href="http://www.bungie.net/7_Destiny-BETA---Early-2014/en/News/News?aid=11125" target="_blank">Bungie has officially announced</a> there will be a beta test of their next major game release,&nbsp;<em>Destiny</em>, and it will be available to anyone who preorders the game at participating retailers.</p>
<p>The <em>Destiny</em> Beta will start in early 2014.&nbsp; You will need to preorder the game and register your beta code at <a title="Destiny Beta Site" href="http://www.destinythegame.com/beta" target="_blank">the Official Destiny Website</a> to receive beta access&nbsp;on your respective platform.&nbsp; The beta will be run by Bungie and will be available for the Xbox 360, Xbox One, Playstation 3 and Playstation 4 versions of the game.</p>
<p><em>Destiny</em> is coming in 2014 to multiple platforms including Xbox 360, Playstation 3, Playstation 4 and Xbox One.</p>
