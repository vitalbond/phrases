<div><p>Nintendo has posted up the latest Nintendo Direct.&nbsp; In it you can find the latest previews for Nintendo&rsquo;s upcoming games on the Wii U and 3DS, including <em>Super Mario 3D World</em>, <em>Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds</em>, and <em>Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze</em>.&nbsp; There are also some major announcements made on upcoming games, like the latest confirmed character for the next <em>Super Smash Bros</em> game.</p>

<p>Personally, while it&rsquo;s great to see these new games,&nbsp;I&rsquo;m most happy for the announced updates to Nintendo Zone.&nbsp; Instead of just having one StreetPass tag with the most recent user who visited the Nintendo Zone Location, you&rsquo;ll be able to get up to six StreetPass Tags.&nbsp; This will make StreetPass enabled games like <em>Warrior&rsquo;s Way</em>, <em>Find Mii</em>, and <em>Mii Force</em> a lot easier to complete, especially if it is hard to find other 3DS owners on a daily basis.</p>
