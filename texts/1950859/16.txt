<div>An accident between two tractor-trailer trucks resulted in three injuries and closed the Route 219 Davidsville exit for several hours on Wednesday. The Richland Township accident closed the northbound exit late into the evening. The exit was closed at 9 p.m., according to Cambria County Control. The accident happened at about 3 p.m. near the McNally Bridge. Three people were injured and transported to Conemaugh Medical Memorial Center, Johnstown. State police, Richland Township police and the Richland and Jerome fire departments responded to the scene. This photograph was taken using a panoramic lens.</div>
