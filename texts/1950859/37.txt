<div>2013 is the tenth year for the Coleman Station Bluegrass Festival and it started Friday with six different bands playing for a large crowd of fans. Blue Shades, a group from the Ligonier area was the second act to play. They are Ray Bruckman on fiddle, Troy Wigfield on bass, John Dively on banjo and Brad Foust playing guitar. The festival continues on Saturday with seven different bands performing from noon until 10 PM.</div>
