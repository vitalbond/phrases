<div>The thirteenth annual Stoystown Lions Antique Tractor Festival started on Thursday.  The festival runs rain or shine August 1, 2, 3 and 4 with this years featured tractor being Allis Chalmers. Events include tractor pulls, music, a wide variety of food, parades and "Roof Garden Tractor Buddies" square dancing tractors on Sunday at 12:30.  Lee Shaffer of Boswell, a retired John Deere mechanic, restored this 1977 400 John Deere.It is one of the many tractors on display.</div>
